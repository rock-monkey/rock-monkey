Birds

Birds are the only animals in the whole world that can fly. Apart from Bats, Geese, Ducks, Flies, Butterflies, WingedAnts, Bluebottles, Dragonflies, certain varieties of Pigs (but not GuineaPigs) and Humans.
They live in nests made of sticks and leaves and lay Eggs in them. This is because Birds have not invented houses. Sometimes Humans build bird houses for them to live in, though this practice went into decline after GordonBrown declared that bird houses were liable for CouncilTax.
The collective term for Birds is a Flap.
