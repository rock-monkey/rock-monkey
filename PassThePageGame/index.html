Pass the Page

Remember in School when you used to get a piece of paper and fold it over and then you'd each write a piece of a story, without seeing what the person before you had written?
It always ended up being a story about Uncle Jim and Aunt Jemima having sex with a turkey in space while being shot at by a cowboy...
This is just like that, but using futuristic technology!
I couldn't think of a name for it so I've called it 'Pass the Page'
How we do it:
Click here to find the first page of the first 'Pass the Page' (the idea is we can do some more. You'll find a list of the parts of the story. Go to the first one that is as-yet-unwritten and add your bit of the story (only do one page, leave some for everyone else). Then read/write-protect it. When all the pages are complete everyone can unprotect the pages (or Dan can make a program that'll do it for us) and we can read the completed story!
Another fantstic use of the internet.
