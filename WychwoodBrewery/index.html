Wychwood Brewery

Wychwood Brewery make Hob Goblin, Circle Master, FiddlersElbow and Goliath, all of which are very nice types of Real Ale. People in AberWorld can have difficulty getting Hob Goblin, but FiddlersElbow is very popular in The Flat come Troma Night.

Mister J T A once looked all over their website - http://www.wychwood.co.uk/ - in the hope that he could find one of their 'Largerboy' posters in the shop (which shows the legendary Hob Goblin clutching a pint of the eponymous brew and demanding "What's the matter, Lager Boy, afraid you might taste something?") and couldn't find one.

As a result, Mister J T A e-mailed the lovely people of the Wychwood Brewery, and they sent him two Hob Goblin posters for free.

So go buy their beer!
