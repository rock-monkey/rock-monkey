Nena

Nena was a one-hit wonder in 1983 with the English version of 99RedBalloons.

Unless, that is, you count the German releases.

As a leading figure in the Mid-80's 'Deutsche Welle' (along with 'Die Prinzen' and 'Die Toeten Hoesen'), Nena had hits in the German chart with:
Leuchtturm;
Irgendwie, Irgendwo, Irgendwann;
and Nur Getraumt. (amongst others)

If Dan would like a copy of 'Nena- Die Band' (a Greatest Hits collection), he should ask Simon.
