RockMonkey Personality Test: Question 7

You've really screwed up this time. You've done something so bad, your other half may never forgive you. Something so disgustingly insensitive I can't even bring myself to type it. How do you make it all better?

a) Pretend it was your long-lost twin who committed the terrible offence

b) Stick something in the nukerowave, dig out some candles and hope that food will win forgiveness.

c) Leave the country and never come back. Better that than being murdered as you sleep!

d) Buy everything in Thorntons, 100 tonnes of wrapping paper and a couple of crates of champagne.

e) Say your sorry. And mean it. If they can't take the apology, then you'll never win them back.
