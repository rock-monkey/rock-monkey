LiberalDemocrats
Seem to be making the most sense. They're running a positive campaign, they've kept a constant view on the war in Iraq (more on that later) and they're the only party who seem to be promising that they'll increase public services by increasing your taxes. They were opposed to the war in Iraq (as I was) however they pledge that if they are elected they will bring the troops home quickly. This is not a good thing to do. The UK was involved in destroyiong the infrastructure ofg Iraq; they are now duty-bound to remain and bear the associated costs untill that infrastructure can be restored in a way of which the Iraqi people approve.
Also, the last time they were in power they increased the democracy of the country: they gave women the vote.
