BigShinyTorch

Wooo! Shiny thing!
A BigShinyTorch is a useful accesory to have if you are going for a walk in the dark. Although, to be honest, if you're going for a walk in the dark you won't be able to tell if it's shiny so you might as well have a BigDullTorch. Unless somebody else comes along with a BigShinyTorch and shines it at your BigDullTorch, thus eclipsing you and making you feel like the worthless scum you are. Why on Earth didn't you think of that and mke sure you took your BigShinyTorch instead of your BigDullTorch, eh?
