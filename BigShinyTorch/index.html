Big Shiny Torch

Wooo! Shiny thing!
A Big Shiny Torch is a useful accesory to have if you are going for a walk in the dark. Although, to be honest, if you're going for a walk in the dark you won't be able to tell if it's shiny so you might as well have a Big Dull Torch. Unless somebody else comes along with a Big Shiny Torch and shines it at your Big Dull Torch, thus eclipsing you and making you feel like the worthless scum you are. Why on Earth didn't you think of that and mke sure you took your Big Shiny Torch instead of your Big Dull Torch, eh?
