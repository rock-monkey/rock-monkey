Just so you can begin to appreciate quite how awful it is:
  • The entire audio track was recorded seperately and then badly ADRed by just three people
  • A lead actress broke her leg during casting and rather than re-cast her role they simply re-wrote the script at the last minute to ensure that she didn't ever need to stand up or have her legs seen on screen
  • The opening quarter hour is a shot of a car driving around a nondescript highway, with bad continuity, because they forgot to put the credits on
  • At the premiere, the audience began throwing things at the screen, and the cast and crew had to run to escape the mob
  • After the release, the guy playing Torgo committed suicide, although to be fair he was suffering from clinical depression before the film was even made
    It must be said, that this is the worst film I have ever seen. I couldn't sit through BarbWire, but fuck... I wouldn't have been able to sit through this either if I hadn't got people egging me on and the MysteryScienceTheatre guys taking the piss out of it so well. - DanHuntley
