Promenade

You are on the promenade, outside The Bay Hotel, a happy bouncey nightclub which tries to pass off paint stripper as vodka. An underpass leads round to the back of the hotel. To the North, Constitution Hill can be seen, overshadowing this part of the town. To the South, the promenade continues past the bandstand and towards the pier. The beach lies West.
What next?
  • North, towards Constitution Hill
  • South, towards the pier
  • Through the underpass
  • Down onto the beach
