Splurg

The Splurg is the Mortal Enemy and ancient protaganist of the Fleeble Widget. Very little is known about its habitat, breeding habits or anatomy, save that it is the one creature in the whole of existence to be truly two-dimensional.
This allows it to sneak up on its prey by turning sideways so that it is invisible, then suddenly turning to face its hapless victim, which is then scared to death by the Splurg's terrifying spiky visage and implausibly jagged mouth.
The Splurg, having no digestive organs, is then unable to eat its prey.
If there is a point to all this, it is yet to be discovered.
