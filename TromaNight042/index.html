Troma Night Adventure

North Beach

North beach is long and pebbley, and liberally dotted with breakwaters and a jetty. Looking Eastward, you can see the promenade, and the lights of Terrace Road leading towards the town centre. The beach continues Southwest, towards the pier, and to the North, towards Constitution Hill.
