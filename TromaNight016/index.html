Troma Night Adventure

North Parade

You are on North Parade, a pair of filtered one-way lanes heading East towards the foot of Penglais Hill. Thespian Street leads off to the South, towards Stanley Road and Holy Trinity Church. To the North, Morfa Mawr runs past Scholars to the back of The Bay, and beyond, to Constitution Hill. Aberystwyth town centre lies to the West.
What next?
