The Fiery Furnaces

The Fiery Furnaces are a brother and sister duo Matthew and Eleanor Friedberger. They make wordy countrified garage rock with added synth and weirdness.
Much beloved of Restless Boy, there are a number of references to them in his WikiGame The Revenge Of Zanatoth.

These include:
Quay Cur, Blueberry Boat, Chief Inspector Blancheflower, Birdie Brain, and Straight Street are all FF song titles.

Obviously Captain Eleanor Friedberger and her dog Matthew are named after the band. Their album 'Blueberry Boat' includes a song called 'My dog was lost but now he's found', in which the lost mutt is 'found' in religious terms in a church. As Matthew the dog is in The Revenge Of Zanatoth.

In addition Ethan Appleday in The Revenge Of Zanatoth informs the player that they look 'trouble trouble worried' which is a line from a song from the FF's first album 'Gallowsbird's Bark'.
