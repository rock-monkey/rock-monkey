UnitedNations

The UN is a multi-national intergovernmental organisation dealing with thousands of issues which affect a multitude of people around the globe. From health to education to simply food and water the UN is an important player on the global stage specifically related to humanitarian issues.
It has however come under lots of pressure of late due to the foreign policies of some states and internal scandals.
Is famous for messing up in Rwanda and sending in a small group of Dutch soldiers with small arms against a mass of people with tanks, mortars and other such weapons to protect part of the population in the break up of Yugoslavia.
Is also famous for inspiring humanitarian intervention, the world food programme, the millennium development goals and keeping the peace in various parts of the planet.
Like everything t'is good and is bad.
