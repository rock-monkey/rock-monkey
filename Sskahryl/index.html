Sskahryl

Sskahryl is a Gold Dragon currently residing at NaNeis, where the Samaritans shop used to be. Although for some reason he is coloured black. This may be due to him being outdoors for long periods of time. The nice lady at NaNeis told me he has spend a lot of time as an outdoor water feature, which I found somewhat disturbing...
The nice lady at NaNeis now knows that we seek the release of Sskahryl, so it's only a matter of time now..
