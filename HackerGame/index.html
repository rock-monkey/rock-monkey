HackerGame

Hacker is a custom card game by SteveJacksonGames. It features a particularly complicated ruleset, and some horrendous in-game mathematics (have a MathMonkey around whenever you're playing it!), but it's still a great game. In the game, players (hackers) have to try to gain access to as many systems as possible - systems are represented by cards, each with potential connections on their 'edges' to other nearby systems. Co-operation is encouraged to a high degree ("I'll give you the information you need to hack into ThePentagon if you give me your Hackintosh!"), but backstabbing is common.
DanHuntley thinks that the game would make a fantastic multiplayer computer game, and keeps promising to have a go at making it. However, as he's always promising such things, he's got far too many projects on the go to finish any given one of them.
