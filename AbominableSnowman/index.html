Abominable Snowman

Far larger version of the more-frequently spotted "Bominable Snowmen" that children the world over are used to seeing, the Abominable Snowman is a monsterous snow-creature that eats GuineaPigs for breakfast.
Taste like Lemons. Not to be confused with Lesbians. Rarely found outside of Daventry.
