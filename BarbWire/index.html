Barb Wire: Don't call me babe!
Thoroughly awful film starring Pamela Anderson as a biker in the far future who drives around shooting at men and saying her catchphrase "Don't call me babe."
The film opens with a silhouetted shot of Ms. Anderson taking a shower, and becomes rapidly worse thereafter. Dan has tried to watch the film on two seperate occassions and has had to give up on both.
Not to be confused with Barbed Wire.
