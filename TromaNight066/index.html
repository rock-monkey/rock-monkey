Penglais Hill

You are at the top of Penglais Hill. Looking down into the town, you can make out the headlights of the cars moving around, dipping in and out of Aberystwyth's contorted one-way system.

On the North side of the road is an entrance to Penglais nature reserve, and a path to PJM, the student village. More familiar to you, on the South side is the entrance to Penglais Campus, the main campus of the University Of Wales Aberystwyth.
