RockMonkey

Space

(See also TheUniverse)
Side project of JimmyCauty (of TheKLF). Produced a one-off ambient-style album in 1990 called, creatively "Space". Think "ThePlanets", but with synthesizers. Now ridiculously hard to get hold of, but bootlegs are freely available
When Jimmy left TheOrb in 1990 to work with TheKLF full time, he took the tapes with him and released them on the KLF label
