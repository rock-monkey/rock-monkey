RockMonkey

Space

(See also The Universe)

Side project of Jimmy Cauty (of TheKLF). Produced a one-off ambient-style album in 1990 called, creatively "Space". Think "ThePlanets", but with synthesizers. Now ridiculously hard to get hold of, but bootlegs are freely available

When Jimmy left The Orb in 1990 to work with TheKLF full time, he took the tapes with him and released them on the KLF label
