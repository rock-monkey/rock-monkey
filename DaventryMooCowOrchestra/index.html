DaventryMooCowOrchestra

Famous orchestra from Daventry. The musicians and instruments are all MooCows. Some play the violin others are used as percussion by a human who finely tunes their udders by allowing them to fill with milk the letting it out until it is at the correct pitch, the banging them with a hammer.
They do particularly good cowbell solos.
