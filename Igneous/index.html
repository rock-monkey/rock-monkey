Igneous

Igneous is an IRC-bot who looks after the RockMonkey Chat Room. He's rather stupid at the moment, but he'll learn. Some of his better quotes can be found on the Chat Room Quotes page.

He seems to have a crush on Sian Thomas (chocorifi), ever since she proposed to him. Restless Boy's gonna give him serious beats if he ever sees him...

It's main purpose is to sit there quietly and log channel activity for the statistics. However it seems to be out to drive BrynS and JonA mad.
