ASeriesofUnfortunateEvents

Fiendishly addictive series of childrens books by Lemony Snicket. Follows the miserable lives of the Baudelaire Siblings who have been left apparently orphaned by a big fire, the stories continue as they try to escape the clutches of the evil Count Olaf.

Everyone who gets introduced to it is hooked. Except for Mister J T A who read three and thought they were a bit repetitive... thereby missing the point where the series takes a turn and gets more involved and interesting. Intelligenter people say they're much cleverer than Harry Potter. Restless Boy wishes he'd had this when he was a child. Me too (Alec R) what can the significance of the Sugar Bowl be?

Now onto its eleventh book (of 13). Recently adapted as a film starring Jim Carrey which was horrendous.
