AndysRecords

AndysRecords is a now-defunct chain of independent record stores which tended to cater to the 'Dad' market.
It is also the name of a record shop in Aberystwyth which has no links to the chain.
Run by sarcastic and midly unpleasant AndyDavis who made the headlines for refusing to pay his Phonographic Performance Ltd licence to allow him to play music in his shop.
He employs CaiStrachen, and Elin who runs KnitClub.
