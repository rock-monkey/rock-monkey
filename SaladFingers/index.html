SaladFingers

A cartoon by DavidFirth about a lonely masochist and his adventures in a hostile world. His tendency to terrify or unintentionally kill all prospective friends is a great source of laughter or sadness to fans (depending on their disposition). His hobbies include holding picnics, stroking his nipples with stinging nettles, talking to insects, touching rusty things, drawing his own blood and dreaming of one day travelling to France.
He has a collection of imaginary friends to talk to. These include;
Hubert Cumberdale
Jeremy Fisher
Margery Stewart-Baxter
