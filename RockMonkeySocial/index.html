The 1st Bi-Annual RockMonkey Social
This idea came about during the last few weekends. The theme will be 'TromaNightAdventure'. The plan so far:
  • Figure out a list of all the places in TromaNightAdventure.
  • Figure out a route which takes in all of the above.
  • Figure out which pubs are on that route.
  • Go to all those pubs and drink.
  • Go to all the locations in the game, and take photos.
  • Upload the photos somewhere, and put them into the game.
Does anyone have any preference about when this should be? Who is coming?
  • JonAtkinson would come (because it was his idea). And I don't really mind when.
  • DanHuntley is in (he's behind TromaNightAdventure, so he'd love to be involved!)
  • AndyKeohane will be there (he is the RockMonkey after all).
