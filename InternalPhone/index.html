RockMonkey

Internal Phone

Looks like a regular (if old) Land Line, but you can only call a few people, such as the emergency services (222 I think), Penbryn Halls Helpful Porters (2900) and Nightline (1717).

Has the University Crest on it.

Not to be confused with the Infernal Phone.
