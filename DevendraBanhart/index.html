Devendra Banhart


Devendra Banhart is an American Singer/songwriter with more than a passing musical/vocal resemblence to the late, great, MarcBolan. He has released three albums 'Oh Me Oh My... the Way the Day Goes By the Sun Is Setting Dogs Are Dreaming Lovesongs of the Christmas Spirit', 'Rejoicing in the Hands (of the Golden Empress)' and 'Nino Rojo'. He sings skewed lyrics in a variety of bizarre voices and plays wonderful fingerpicked guitar of a kind not seen since Nick Drake.
Best album; Rejoicing in the Hands.
