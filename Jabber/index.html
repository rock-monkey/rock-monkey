Jabber

Jabber is an open-source instant messaging protocol.
Why should you give a shit?
Because Jabber can be used as the 'glue' to translate messages from, say, MSN Messenger to ICQ. Or from AIM to Yahoo. Or just about anything.
This means that there should be no more moaning about what IM service to use. Everyone can get a Jabber client (suggest: Gossip for Linux, Exodus for Windows, Nitro for MacOSX), set it up, and then everyone can talk to everyone regardless of their preferred brand of proprietary lock-in.
