Tsunami

Not a Tidal Wave.


An ocean wave with a long period that is formed by an underwater earthquake, landslide, or volcanic eruption. It may travel unnoticed across deep open water for thousands of miles from its point of origin and builds up to great heights over shallower water. Also known as a seismic sea wave.
