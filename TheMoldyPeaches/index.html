The Moldy Peaches

(The following paragraph is shamelessly stolen from Wikipedia)
TheMoldyPeaches are a self-proclaimed anti-folk group, consisting of, essentially, Adam Green and Kimya Dawson, with the addition of bassist Steve Mertens, drummer Strictly Beats, and guitarists Jack Dishel and Toby Goodshank. Green and Dawson met at Exile on Main Street Records in Mount Kisco, New York, and began working together afterwards. In the early 1990s, the group was part of the New York City underground scene, partially due to the band's strange costumes, reminiscent of The Frogs (Kimmy typically dresses as a rabbit, Adam as Robin Hood - Kt). Touring along with bands such as The Strokes and Tenacious D, The Moldy Peaches have released two albums with Sanctuary Records.
