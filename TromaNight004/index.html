Corner Of Lloyds TSB

You are on Terrace Road, at the corner of Lloyds TSB bank (where Terrace Road meets CambrianPlace). Burger King is a hive of activity, with kids running around as the drug pushers and burger-flippers alike try to make their money. To the West is Cambrian Place, and - of course - The Flat. To the South is Alexandra Terrace: you can see Wetherspoons clearly. To the North lies the centre of Aberystwyth, Great Darkgate Street.
What next?
  • Wander down Cambrian Place
  • Head towards Alexandra Terrace and Wetherspoons
  • Go North, to Great Darkgate Street
  • Enter Burger King
