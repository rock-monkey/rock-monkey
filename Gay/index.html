Gay

Word with many possible meanings in popular culture, aside from it's traditional meaning of "happy". These include:

1. A HomoSexual (especially a male HomoSexual).
2. To be HomoSexual.
3. To act in a manner befitting the stereotype of a HomoSexual man. Typically derogatory.
4. I remember Lemons.

Compare Lesbians.
Paul Mann wishes to point out that 2 and 3 are both adjectives, not nouns, and 1 just sounds odd if used as a noun (See Little Britain)
Dan Huntley concurs, and has removed the word "Noun", but disagrees that 1 "sounds odd as a noun": examples - "See that gay?" "I'm a gay," "You gay."
Paul Mann replies, noting that whist it may be (fairly) common usage, he still thinks it's very bad grammar
