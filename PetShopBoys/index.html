Pet Shop Boys

Synthpop duo comprised of Neil Tennant and Chris Lowe
Hits include "West End Girls", "What Have I Done To Deserve This?", "It's a Sin", "Always on My Mind (cover)", "Heart", "Go West". Then they committed career suicide in America and haven't been the same since
Best Pet Shop Boys songs include "King's Cross", "Being Boring", "DJ Culture", "I Want a Dog", "In Denial", "Radiophonic", "Go West" and "Heart (album version)"
Not as good as Erasure, but strangely more popular even though lately the latter have been scoring more chart hits... The PSB do have more number ones, however
