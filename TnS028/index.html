Western Gate

"What queen? What are you talking about?" You ask, totally confused.

"You're an outsider, aren't you?" The elf asks. You refrain from pointing out that the fact you're a squirrel should have made this obvious.

"You should leave now. This is no place for anybody cute or fluffy. Go!"

He gestures at you wildly until you feel so disorientated you can't bear to stand there any more.
What now?
