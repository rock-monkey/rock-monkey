Ducks

Quacking water-based fowl. Subsist only on donations of stale bread from TheGeneralPublic.
The most popular Duck of all time is DonaldDuck, who was the captain of the Titanic before going on to star in his on Cartoon series.
The least popular Duck of all time was the unnamed Mallard whose mournful quacking in a partially submerged tube caused 2004's Asian Tsunami. (see ChaosTheory)
Ducks are sometimes mistaken for Birds
The collective term for Ducks is a Twaddle.
