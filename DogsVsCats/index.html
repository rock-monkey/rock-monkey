Dogs versus Cats

This is a page to discuss which is better Cats or Dogs
When you have considered the points, you can vote here
Reasons Cats are superior

  • When you're in a bad mood, they don't try to cheer you up - DanHuntley.
  • They smell less awful - DanHuntley. (not true, my dad's Cat Harry farts like a human being after a big curry - RestlessBoy)
  • In almost every case, they're more than capable of fending for themselves, should an emergency arise that requires them to.
  • They're so efficient they can get away with sleeping for two thirds of their life.
  • They come before Dogs in the alphabet.
  • They're not stupid enough to be whipped into pulling a sledge through snow.
  • Cats purr.
  • Cats rub your leg when they want affection, not when they're horny.
  • Cats use a litter box. Dogs use your leg.
  • Cats always land on their feet.
  • Cats look cute sleeping on the TV. Dogs just crash right in front of the screen.
  • Fewer cat owners suffer from 'Flappy Tail' lacerations than dog owners.
  • No one has ever had to "Beware of the Cat".
  • They're independent. If you forget to feed them they'll do fine.
