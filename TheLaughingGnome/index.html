TheLaughingGnome

The kind of record that could ruin your career if you're not careful. Catchy, but releasing it as a single was a terrible mistake as it doesn't represent his (DavidBowie) typical style of music. (It was only released as a single after he found fame with his later career, it was not a single when he was doing the 'David Bowie' album stuff. Hence the sleeve usually, misleadingly featuring a picture of Ziggy Stardust)
Features a mostly CallResponse duet between Bowie and a high-pitched chuckling gnome (also Bowie) who lives in his house and makes him a fortune by writing "comedy prose for radio shows"
PaulMann owns this on 45 vinyl and is not ashamed
