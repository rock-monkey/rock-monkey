The Orb

Ambeint-house group focusing around Dr Alex Patterson. Sometime member Jimmy Cauty is also in TheKLF.

Their most famous song is 'Little Fluffy Clouds'. They famously released a single, 'The Blue Room', which was 39 minutes long. When they appeared on Top Of The Pops to promote it, instead of miming the music they simply sat and played chess.

Another famous Orb song is 'The huge ever growing pulsating brain that rules from the centre of the ultraworld'.
