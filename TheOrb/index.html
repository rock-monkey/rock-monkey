TheOrb

Ambeint-house group focusing around DrAlexPatterson. Sometime member JimmyCauty is also in TheKLF.
Their most famous song is 'Little Fluffy Clouds'. They famously released a single, 'The Blue Room', which was 39 minutes long. When they appeared on TopOfThePops to promote it, instead of miming the music they simply sat and played chess.
Another famous Orb song is 'The huge ever growing pulsating brain that rules from the centre of the ultraworld'.
