Irate Welsh Fishmonger

Standing outside the fishmongers, you can see a grubby chalkboard. It says "Sorry No Mackerel". Guide dogs aren't mentioned.

It smells faintly of fish.

You see a Dirty Washing Up Sponge in the gutter. It looks very tatty and worn.
