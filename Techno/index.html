Techno

The best kind of Dance Music

Almost always in 4/4 time with a BPM of one of the following: 96/110/120/125/133/135/140/160

Typically carries a back-beat. A repetitive bass-line alternating with the drum beat used to be called HiNrg, but that phrase has fallen out of use since the mid-90s

Earned a bad reputation largely thanks to 2 Unlimited, who bastardised it into something Bubblegum Pop listeners could cope with ("Techno techno techno techno")

The US seem to have a very strange idea of techno, and to them it is what would we would refer to as SynthPop or Electronica

Examples under the US definition: Moby, Erasure, Pet Shop Boys, The ThompsonTwins, DepecheMode
Examples under the UK definition: Royksopp, ManhattanClique, Faithless, Fragma, Apoptygma Berzerk and most "hard dance music"

Scooter falls somewhere in between, which is more like Euro Dance

See also Hip Hop
