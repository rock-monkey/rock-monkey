Promenade South


You find yourself on the Southern part of Aberystwyth's coastline - the stretch of the promenade that runs parallel to South Beach. South Beach is only a couple of dozen thousand years - a short time, in geological terms - away from being smooth sand, unlike it's pebbley Northern counterpart. The promenade continues to the Northwest, arcing around the hill on which the Castle sits. To the South lies the marina. Many small roads wind off in a Northeasterly direction, towards the town centre.
What next?
  • Follow the promenade North, to the pier
  • South, to the marina
  • Clamber up to the Castle
  • Walk down onto South Beach
  • Find your way to the town centre
