Promenade

NightAdventure/pierdusk.jpg" alt="Aberystwyth Pier" class="center" />

You are at the junction of Pier Street and Marine Terrace (the promenade). The pier extends out to sea in an West-Northeasterly direction. Pier street runs off to the South, past Hollywood Pizza and towards town. To the Northeast, the prom leads towards the distant Constitution Hill, and to the Southwest, it leads around the war memorial towards South Beach - invisible from here because of the shape of the small hill between here and there that houses the Castle.

Pier Video, Aberystwyth's best video rental shop, stands open on the pier.
What next?
  • Northeast, towards Constitution Hill
  • Southwest, to South Beach
  • South, along Pier Street
  • Up the path to the Castle
  • Enter Pier Video
