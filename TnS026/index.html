Western Gate

"You don't sound very happy." You tell the guard. Your super-honed squirrel senses are telling you that all is not as it seems here.
The guard turns, very slowly. His face is still fixed in the ridiculous grin. He looks you over.
"The Queen must be getting pretty desperate if she has to send rodents to do her dirty work."
The tone is mocking, but he's still grinning as if his life depended on it.
What now?
Get out of there!
Talk:
'I'll rodent you, you lump of lard! And your helmet doesn't fit!'
'What queen? What are you talking about?'
'I'm on your side. I hate the Queen, and have come to free the elves from her reign of terror!'
