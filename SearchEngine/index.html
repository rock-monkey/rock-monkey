SearchEngine

The fearsome black-and-red SearchEngine was one of the principal characters in Thomas the Tank Engine: The Sodor Massacres, a series written by a channel 5 development team which tragically never saw daylight.
In it, an army of evil trains led by SearchEngine took over the island, forcing Thomas and the other survivors to live underground, with only a troop of semi-naked, nymphomanic Lesbians to bring them the supplies they needed.
SearchEngine himself had a laser-guided flamethrower on his back, along with razor-sharp cutting blades extending, Boadiccea style, from his wheels.
The series was pulled in the end so that 5 could make the more crowd-pleasing Sister Act III: The Lesbian Years, and the SearchEngine action figures, of which none were ever made, are now highly collectible.
