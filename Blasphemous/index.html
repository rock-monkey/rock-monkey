Blasphemous

Taking the name of God in vain. All the CoolKids are doing it nowadays.
If you're going to do this properly, be aware that it's actually harder than it sounds. By and large, people seem to agree that shouting "Jesus Buggery Christ" is blasphemy, which is fair enough. But they also seem to think saying "God All-sodding Mighty" is blasphemous, which it isn't.
Jesus' name can be taken in vain, because people know what it is. God's name, however, is some variation on "YWH," with some vowels shoved in. This is because the people who first wrote the name of God didn't want to put the vowels in, because they'd catch Hell for it...
It's probably Iehova. Or Jehova. Or Yaweh. But, frankly, shouting "O Jehova-fiddly-piss!" doesn't quite roll off the tongue.
Trust an all-powerful deity to take all the simplicity out of sin, eh?
