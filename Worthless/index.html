Worthless

adj. for any object, person, activity, individual action, work of art, political decision which:-
i)does not seek to contribute to the happiness and wellbeing of the greatest number of people , and suffering to the fewest, preferably none.
ii)does not seek to progress knowledge, understanding and compassion.
iii)places value on a system (i.e. the CivilService) over those the system is there to serve (i.e. the population).
iv)repeats a previous action of worth for the sole aim of gratifying the person doing it and their immediate group of loved ones - to the detriment of others, or of the above mentioned criteria
