PaveMent


The other great US alt.rock phenomenon of the early nineties along with Nirvana. Never as popular because they didn't sing about killing themselves and being miserable, instead wrote skewedly melodic country tinged rock with wry lyrics and scratchy guitars.
best album to start with is the recently reissued 'Crooked Rain Crooked Rain'.
