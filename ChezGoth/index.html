ChezGoth

ChezGoth is an add-on pack for the SteveJacksonGames card game ChezGeek. DanHuntley does not own a copy.
In ChezGoth, players act as goths and try to get slack points by getting Nookie in crypts, moping around, writing bad poetry etc.
