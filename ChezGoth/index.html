Chez Goth

Chez Goth is an add-on pack for the Steve Jackson Games card game Chez Geek. Dan Huntley does not own a copy.

In Chez Goth, players act as goths and try to get slack points by getting Nookie in crypts, moping around, writing bad poetry etc.
