WarpRecords

Electronica/techno label founded in the late 80s in Sheffield. (website) Began as a label releasing UK pressing of Detroit Techno and New York house before signing UK artists such as LFO, and Seefeel. Has recently expanded to include nominally 'indie' bands such as Broadcast, folk act Gravenhurst, and jazz releases from filmmaker VincentGallo
Artists on Warp include:-
AphexTwin
BoardsOfCanada
Squarepusher
Autechre
Prefuse73
JamieLidell
An excellent resource for Warp material is their downloads website bleep.com
An excellent introduction to Warp is the WarpVision DVD.
