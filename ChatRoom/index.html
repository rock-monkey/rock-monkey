Using The ChatRoom

This is the RockMonkey ChatRoom. If you're going to be hanging around on RockMonkey for hours on end, why not open this RockMonkey WikiPage in a seperate WebBrowser tab (or window, if you're using an inferior WebBrowser), and keep in touch with other RockMonkey users.
Yes; it's IRC, which means that the usual commands work:
  • Use "/nick newname to change your name.
  • Use "/msg otherperson secret message" to send a message to another person.
  • Use "/quit Gotta go wash my socks" to quit, sending a logoff message to the room
  • If you want to connect with a 'traditional' IRC client, point it at a Freenode server (e.g. orwell.freenode.net), channel """#RockMonkey"""

    Igneous

    Igneous (or "Iggy", as he's known to his friends) is a robot who hangs around in the ChatRoom. He's talkative, but a little stupid. Help him learn things by telling him about them (and ask him about them later!). He also helps collect the ChatRoomStats.
