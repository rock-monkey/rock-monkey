RockMonkey Personality Test: Question 3

Something very exciting is happening, like, I don't know, a meteorite has just hit the new Interpol building or something. The whole place is crawling with the press, and sundry excited onlookers. Where are you?
a) Right out at the forefront. You live for moments like this!
b) Exiting the scene before the police can catch up with you...
c) Composing a letter of complaint to your MP. This is supposed to be a quiet area.
d) Sleeping. If people want your attention, they should do this sort of thing after 3pm.
e) Selling things to the crowd. Somebody's going to make money from this, why shouldn't it be you?
