Razor Wire

Razor Wire is a particularly vicious variant of Barbed Wire which is used to guard the top of fences and discourage humans from climbing them. Rather than a series of distributed sharp barbs, Razor Wire is sharp along it's entire surface, making it almost impossible to find a safe point to grip. It is significantly harder to errect than Barbed Wire, and protective gauntlets are required.
