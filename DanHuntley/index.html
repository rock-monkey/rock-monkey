Dan's Presence On RockMonkey

WikiGames and the WikiGameToolkit

DanHuntley made the following WikiGames:
  • WikiPlatformGame and EvilWikiPlatformGame: turn-based platform game fun.
  • WikiBridgePuzzle: at the time of it's development, the biggest wiki game ever: a puzzle game.
  • TromaNightAdventure, an adventure game based on TromaNight and powered by the developmental WikiGameToolkit. Go play it!
  • KnightmareWikiGame is under development, a graphical WikiGame.
  • TromaNightAdventure2 is in the planning stages of development.
  • He's also continuously developing and improving on the WikiGameToolkit, to help others write WikiGames.

    Other Projects

  • He keeps an eye on the RockMonkey UsageStatistics, and reports on the funniest ones.
  • He made Rockipedia, a rock identification tool.
  • Along with PaulMann, he does a reasonable amount of wiki maintenance, including the development of the BadPages section. He's currently working on the OrphanedPagesProblem.
  • He wrote various plugins for the wiki engine, including BadPages, the improved category system, the ChatRoom engine, and parts of Igneous, the ChatRoom bot.
