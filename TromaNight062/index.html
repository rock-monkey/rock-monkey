The Marina

NightAdventure/harbourbtx.jpg" alt="Aberystwyth Marina" class="center" />
You're stood on the bar: a concrete waterbreak that forms an artificial harbour for Aberystwyth marina. At it's far end is a small automated lighthouse with a ladder up the side. Waves crash against the side of the bar and around into the harbour. The lines clatter against the masts of the sailboats as they're caught in the breeze, chaotically whipping music together; like a set of windchimes fighting a storm.
