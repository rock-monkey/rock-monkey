Hell

Every religion seems to have it's own idea of what hell is (except, of course, for those which do not have a concept of hell), but they tend to agree that's it's not a nice place to be, and you'll go there if you don't believe in everything they say.

Typical definitions of hell include things like fire and brimstone, demons with sharp pointy sticks and lava. Of course, they don't take Masochists into account...

Synonyms of hell include: G(e)henna, The Neverworld, Sheol (kind of), Hades, Rhyl, The Infernal Depths, That Place That's Really Really Hot, The Underworld (kind of), branwens restaurant
