Western Gate


"I'll rodent you, you lump of lard!" You cry, pulling your de-squidgilator from your utility belt, "And your helmet doesn't fit!"

You aim the de-squidgilator at the elf, and twist the dial to 'skinny'.

As you are about to push the button, somebody grabs you from behind. A hand is thrust over your mouth, and then something hits you just behind your left ear and the world goes black.
