Bubblegum Pop

A harsh name for pop-music designed to be easily chewable yet have no real substance. Get it? It's clever. Note that there is no such thing as Bubblegum Rock/Metal/Dance. It's still called Bubblegum Pop

A general guideline is that if they're very concerned about chart placement and/or they practically disappeared when they fell out of the charts and/or they don't write their own material then they're probably Bubblegum Pop. This does not mean they're only in it for the money. In fact, some artists like TheKLF deliberately produce Bubblegum Pop in order to fund their RealMusic for the fans. This list may upset some people. Just because somebody is Bubblegum Pop does not mean you can't enjoy it. In fact, this list was mostly generated from my CD collection (shame!). I'm also not counting One Hit Wonders or one-off collaberations, since they didn't have the time to prove they were bubblegum.
