Quidditch

Game played by Harry Potter. You fly about on broomsticks and try to score goals by knocking a ball through a hoop.
Unless you're Harry Potter, you might as well not bother, because if the bespectacled berk has done his job properly, he's just caught a little yellow ball worth 150 points, which rather knackers the "team" element of the game.
