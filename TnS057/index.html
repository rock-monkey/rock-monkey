Marketplace

The elf's hand appears above the stall, clutching his stick. Whilst he is still flailing around, you grab the piece of obvious junk. It fits up your sleeve quite easily, slim enough that it doesn't show through your skin-tight lycra super-squirrel outfit.

The old elf appears on the other side of the stall, glances over the table but doesn't seem to notice that the junk is gone, and then smiles at you. "Now then. What is it that you want, young squirrel?"
