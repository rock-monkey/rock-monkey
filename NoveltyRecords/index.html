Novelty Records

Novelty Records. Typically one-hit wonders. Worse, they often have the intention of *being* one-hit wonders, so they scar you for life and then vanish without a trace. The main way to tell the difference is that Novelty Record makers don't usually take it too seriously, and don't do this sort of thing for a day job (The Laughing Gnome excluded). Here is a rough guide:
  • Don't have an existing musical career
  • Don't release an album
  • Begins with "The"
  • Is a collaberation
  • Is a charity event
  • Is humourous
  • Is a Christmas song
  • Is a football song
  • Is from Europe
  • Is largely instrumental
  • Is based on a cult phenomenon (usually from the Internet)
  • Is very succesful
Answer three or more and it's probably a Novelty Record rather than a one-hit wonder. Note - not exact science, your mileage may vary
See also One Hit Wonders (duh)
