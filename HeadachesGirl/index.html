Headaches Girl

A song by Pagan Wanderer Lu
Inspired by the Bolton Evening News' front page coverage of the death of his friend's sister. A childhood migraine sufferer who died inexplicably in her sleep whilst at university. She was 20 years old. The story was covered in a typically hack style without the consent or contribution of her mother.
