Chez Grunt

Chez Grunt is an add-on to Steve Jackson Games' card game Chez Geek. In this add-on, players are in the army and special rules come in to effect, such as those regarding keeping illicit things ("Thing (Illicit)") in your bunkhouse. It can be played independently of the main Chez Geek set, or with it (in which case the players are all reservists).
