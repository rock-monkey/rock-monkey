ChezGrunt

ChezGrunt is an add-on to SteveJacksonGames' card game ChezGeek. In this add-on, players are in the army and special rules come in to effect, such as those regarding keeping illicit things ("Thing (Illicit)") in your bunkhouse. It can be played independently of the main ChezGeek set, or with it (in which case the players are all reservists).
