Squarepusher

Artist name of Tom Jenkinson (also known as ChaosAD). Makes insane jazz/drum n bass electronica for Warp Records. Has made many individual tracks that are utterly stunning in their inventiveness but has also released a whole bunch of crap as well.

His best album is 'Big Loada' but his best songs '(Do you know) Squarepusher' and 'My Red Hot Car' are to be found elsewhere.

On no account listen to 'Music is rotted one note' as it sounds like three different jazz bands falling down three different sets of spiral stairs and trying to carry on playing, yet all playing different songs - only crap.

a Warp Records artist
