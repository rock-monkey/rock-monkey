CambrianPlace


You find yourself on CambrianPlace, near the centre of Aberystwyth. The street is remarkabley quiet. CambrianPlace runs from East to West, from Chalybeate Street to Thespian Street. To the South, a road leads to Alexandra Terrace, and beyond it you can see Somerfield and the railway station.
MGees cafe, Charlies', and the IrateWelshFishmonger are all closed.
What next?
  • Head South, towards Alexandra Terrace
  • Head East, towards Lloyds TSB
  • Head West, towards Chalybeate Street
