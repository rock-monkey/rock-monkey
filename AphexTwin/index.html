AphexTwin

Mental electronica artist, does head manglingly complicated IDM - has been called the New Mozart, mostly by idiots, he is far more like a latter day IgorStravinsky. Also responsible for two of the best music videos ever for 'Come to Daddy' and 'Windowlicker' both directed by ChrisCunningham.
best album - the Richard D James album.
Also likes to hide photos of himself inside his songs - check out "Equation" from the Windowlicker EP on a Spectrograph, logarithmic scale, about 5:27 in.
Currently releasing a series of EPs under the name Analord (referring to analogue rather than the anus).
Also goes under the names AFX, and GAK. Once did a collaboration with the MikeFlowersPops.
Records for WarpRecords, and his own label Rephlex.
