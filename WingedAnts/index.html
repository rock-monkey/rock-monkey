WingedAnts

These are like ants, except that they fly about, with the wings evident from the nomenclature behind their classification.
Having crawled like normal ants for the whole summer, on one, strangely-agreed-upon, usually stormy day, the winged ants take flight for an airborne sex-fest the likes of which are not seen anywhere else in the animal kingdom.
Then, the males die and the females bite off their wings and set up new colonies, with the ultimate purpose of creating thousands more aerial orgy participants.
