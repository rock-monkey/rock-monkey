RockMonkey

2K

Also known as TheKLF, The Justified Ancients Of Mu Mu, The Jams, TheKFoundation, Disco 2000, One World Orchestra and The Timelords. Side Projects include Space, The Orb, Advanced Acoustic Armaments, and Black Smoke

This article is about the music, the stunts and the events of Bill Drummond and Jimmy Cauty using the moniker 2K
1. Fuck the Millenium

A one-off apperance by Bill and Jimmy. Since TheKLF had retired from the music industry, a new name had to be used, and 2K was chosen.

Kind of acid-pop. Title later used by Scooter. Coincidence? I think not!

Anyway, the B-side is "Acid brass". That is what it sounds like and is kind of disturbing...
