Brandon Flowers

Lead singer of The Killers and a beautiful, beautiful man. He is also a mormon and never drinks anything else but Coca-Cola. The song 'Mr Brightside' is a true story about a girlfriend who cheated on him and broke his heart, but happily he now has a lovely fiance (soon to be killed in a fit of rage by me - Kt Errington). It can also be assumed that Mr Brandon Flowers is quite an arrogant man since he wrote the lyrics:
"It's not confidential, I've got potential, a rushing around"
"Destiny is calling me"
and
"I wanna shine on in the hearts of man"
But I love him anyway.
