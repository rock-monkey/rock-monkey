ThingsToDoWhenAwayFromTheInternet

Things you can do if someone has taken away your computer:
1. Take up a new hobby, such as taming ManEatingTigers.
2. Dye your hair green, paint yourself brown and pretend to be a tree.
3. Form interesting sculptures from stationary, such as long, long chains of PaperClips or PhallicSculptures made out of BluTack.
4. Set up a new identity for yourself, one in which you will never have to sit at a computer again. Discard it on the grounds that you would then miss out on the amazing world of the FleebleWidget.
5. Sleep.
