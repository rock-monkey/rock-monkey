Bad Pages No Content

It is considerered bad wiki etiquette to create a Wiki Page which has no content on it whatsoever, or to remove all of the content from an existing Wiki Page. Pages which remain 'undefined' should appear on the 'Wanted Pages' list, but if people 'blank' pages then this doesn't happen, because it is a defined page... albeit a blank one.

Repairing 'blank' pages is even more noble - and more worthy of WikiGnome-dom - than defining Wanted Pages.

If you disagree with the content of a Wiki Page: ammend it with the facts, or append to it your opinion. Blanking WikiPages is very bad form, and it only takes a second for somebody to click the 'History' link and roll back to the original page!
