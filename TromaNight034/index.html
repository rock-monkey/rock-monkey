Promenade

You find yourself on the Aberystwyth Promenade - Marine Terrace - near Phinikki (which is closed for the winter). Waves lap against the beach as the last rays of the sun glimmer on the horizon.

The promenade continues to the Northeast, towards Constitution Hill, and to the Southwest, towards the old college and the castle. The pier is visible to the South. Terrace Road leads South, back into town.
What next?
  • Head back into town
  • Wander Northeast, along the prom
  • Walk Southwest, along the prom
  • Go down onto the beach
