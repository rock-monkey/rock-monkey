Pixies Reunion

Nobody saw the Pixies this year, there was a band performing Pixies songs under the name 'Pixies'. This was a band consisting of people who used to be members of a band going under the name 'Pixies' who recorded the songs originally. However it was not the Pixies. It was a cash cow, moo! By reforming they cheapened everything they'd ever done before. No one wants to see photos of their idols all fat and old and bald, pretending they still feel the darkness. The Pixies were not a stadium band. They did not play stadium songs. Unless you saw them in a dingy club somewhere before Nirvana made them famous and they split up, you did not see the Pixies.
Glad you enjoyed it though.
