Word Monkey

One who, unlike a RockMonkey, studies English, commonly English Literature. (see poetry, books)
The average Word Monkey weighs five foot seven, and is thirteen stone tall. They can eat most household vegetables, enjoy carrots and most red meats and may live for up to seven years, on an extended degree scheme, although their average lifespan is closer to three years, for the common BA Word Monkey, or four years for the purebred MA variety.
Every Word Monkey is bound by the Universal Laws of Pedantry and, in his or her sworn duty as a Pedant must uphold even very stupid regulations of the language. As a result, every Word Monkey is forbidden to have any affiliation with groups promoting the rationing of full stops, commas, those funny little speechmark things on that random key above 'tab' on a keyboard or any similar cause.
Feel free, however, to see what you make of Full Stop Rationing the campaign, unless you are a Word Monkey, in which case you may not break the Sacred Vows for fear of incurring the Evil Punishment Of Doom!
