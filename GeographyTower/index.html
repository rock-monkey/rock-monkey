Geography Tower

That stupid concrete monstrosity the University Of Wales Aberystwyth houses the transgenics, Geography students and the nominally uber intellegent Earth Science Students in, although the majority of these RockMonkies live in cages below.

It's got about as many floors as there are letters in the Greek alphabet, which might explain how come the levels jump from (I think) D to F, without there being an "E" anywhere in between.

Underneath it is A6, a lecture theatre built in early 1989, with the help of government funding in the hope that some students could shelter there when the USSR dropped the Big One...
