ImaginaryBand

A band which may have recorded songs, released records, played gigs, appeared in photographs, or have a website dedicated to them but which does not in the most realist sense... exist. They require at the very least, a name. They should also have an idea behind them. Anything else is a bonus.
Imaginary Bands give you absolute freedom to do whatever you want musically. Or at least talk about doing it. BillDrummond has been in many Imanginary Bands.
Examples of imaginary bands:
TheStylophonics
AllIEverThinkAboutIsDrowning
TheFuckers
My personal favourite: BitchFruit, wait, they are real.....
