Bottom Of Constitution Hill

You are at the bottom of Constitution Hill, which dominates the Northern end of Aberystwyth's skyline. It is possible to go down to the beach from here. alternatively, roads lead south and southeast, to the promenade and around the back of the seafront buildings, respectively. and, it's always possible to climb up the hill. the cliff railway building is locked with a crude padlock. An aging manhole rests inset into the road.
What next?
  • Descend onto the beach
  • Southeast, behind the promenade
  • South, along the promenade
  • Up Constitution Hill
