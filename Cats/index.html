Cats

Cats are widely adored by YoungGirls and OldLadies, who if single must own at least one to qualify for a state pension.
Their popularity is only equalled by the utter contempt shown by Cats to their proponents.
Cats do not play games as Dogs do, being far too aloof. However they enjoy the following activities:
  • Slaying smaller animals and leaving them on the kitchen floor - Cats do this knowing it upsets and disgusts people, aware that they will be forgiven as Humans will attribute it to an attempt to provide sustenance for their beloved family. Inside they are howling with laughter.
  • Exposing their Anuses - Cats' favourite way to show contempt for those who love them is to walk away with their tails high in the air exposing a small pink patch.
Much of the appeal of Cats comes from the fact that they begin life as Kittens.
Cats are the arch rivals or Dogs. The Conflict
The collective term for Cats is a Resentment.
