BeautifulFreak

BeautifulFreak is a song by eels. It's from the album BeautifulFreak, released on August the 13th, 1996. The song also features in the movie 'Shrek2'.
Ha! Got you there! It doesn't actually feature in 'Shrek2' (or 'Shrek' for that matter). eels have contributed a song to each of the Shrek films. They provided My Beloved Monster// for the first film (it plays when Shrek and Fiona are having fun on the walk back from the castle) and an as-yet unreleased song //I Need Some Sleep in the sequel. It, of course, plays when Shrek can't sleep.
