EuroDance

A lighter spin-off of Techno music, popularised in the mid-90s. People may say "It sounds '80s'", Maybe it does, but it's not. Music on proper DanceMachines (ie not those that play pop-music) tends to be EuroDance
EuroDance was practically invented by DaveRodgers
Incidentally, modern JPop is a spin-off of EuroDance
EuroDance artists include:
  • Chilu
  • DaveRodgers
  • DeltaQueens
  • LeslieParrish
  • MegaNrgMan
  • MichikoNeya
  • MickeyB
  • Sara
  • Scooter
