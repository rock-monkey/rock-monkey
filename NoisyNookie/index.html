Noisy Nookie

Noisy Nookie is, as it's name suggests, nookie which is noisy. Nookie noise consists of noises made during the act of sex both by the people involved and by their environemt as a result of their actions. For example,
  • Moans
  • Screams
  • Bedposts rocking and banging against walls
  • Bedspring squeaks
  • Ejaculation into a chip pan
Noisy Nookie is also the name of a special rule in the card game Chez Geek, which can cause players in 'adjacent rooms' to lose 'Sleep' cards.
