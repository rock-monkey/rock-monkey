Depression

A bit of an isidious thing this is. Rather than honestly coming upon you with a gradual build up it just seems to turn up, sit next to you, maybe buy you a whiskey and not talk to you. Every so often it'll glance in your general direction but, like a good friend, it mostly just sits there letting things go unsaid, as you both know the score.

Every so often depression can be a nice little thing to enjoy. Sometimes not.
