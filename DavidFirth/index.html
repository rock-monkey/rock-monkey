RockMonkey

David Firth

A prolific if clearly disturbed cartoonist whose work can be found, among other places at http://www.fat-pie.com/index.html. His most famous cartoons are Salad Fingers and Burnt Face Man but I would also recommend his one-off cartoons Video Dating Tape and Valentine's Day Special to those who are interested.

He's also responsible for Chavs On Film
