Penglais Nature Reserve

You are at the uppermost point of Penglais nature reserve. From here, you can look down upon the entirety of Aberystwyth. You can clearly make out the lights of the pier, the shape of the coastline, and several other key buildings in the town. You can't see The Flat because there's a building in the way, but you know that it's there. You have a very clear view of North Parade, which runs parallel to your line of sight.
