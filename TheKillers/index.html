The Killers

Headed by Mr Brandon Flowers The Killers are fans of 80s British Indie, in spite of the fact that they come from Las Vegas. Their album Hot Fuss is marvellous not least because it includes the tracks Mr Brightside and Somebody Told Me.
It should be noted that the track Glamorous Indie Rock & Roll is in fact ironic - they're taking the piss of bands in America who call themselves indie to justify their depression and lack of songwriting, Brandon said so in an interview. He finds it quite hilarious that British fans have failed to notice this.
