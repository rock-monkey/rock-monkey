Progress

Dictionary.com says this:
"progress n.
1.Movement, as toward a goal; advance.
2.Development or growth: students who show progress.
3.Steady improvement, as of a society or civilization: 'a believer in humans progress.'
4.A ceremonial journey made by a sovereign through his or her realm."

However these days progress seems to mean:

1. Well off westerners making lots more money.
2. There being more pointless stuff for them to buy.
3. Both the above at the expense of people who are unable to make more money or buy pointless things.
4. RealityTV shows.
5. Websites on which you can look at people wearing hats made of meat.
