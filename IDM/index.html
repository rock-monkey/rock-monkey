IDM

short for 'Intelligent Dance Music' - a horrible name for cutting edge electronica which you can't dance to.
Artists referred to as IDM typically include
AphexTwin
BoardsOfCanada
Squarepusher
Autechre
An alternative name, coined by Richard D James is Braindance.
Record labels associated with IDM are WarpRecords and Rephlex
