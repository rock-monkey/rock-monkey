Haiku

Ancient Japanese poetry form. Arguments as to how to write an authentic haiku in english are stupid as you can't. However the generally accepted pratice is three lines. One of five syllables, followed by one seven syllables, followed by one of five.
Traditional haiku are only written about the weather, must contain a colour in the first line, and are thought most apt when ending on a verb. Most western versions do not follow this, because most of our words are polysyllabic.
