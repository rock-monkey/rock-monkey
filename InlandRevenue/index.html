InlandRevenue

In the UK, the InlandRevenue are best known for being responsible for:
  • Collection of IncomeTax
  • Assessment of eligibility for state financial aid
  • Collection of NationalInsurance
  • Regulation of companies and charities (through CompaniesHouse)
