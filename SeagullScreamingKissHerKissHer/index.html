SeagullScreamingKissHerKissHer

Sadly defunct Japanese indie-punk girl duo. did some utterly wonderful simple dumb punk rock records throughout the 90s and were just on the verge of touring the UK when they split which made RestlessBoy want to die. Singer AihaHigurashi released a solo album this year which was a bit more mature and sensible and mostly in japanese.
Best album - Red Talk - a compilation - is the only one available in the UK. all others can be ordered from Japan and the best one is 'Future or No Future'.
