Under The Pier

You tie a heavy rock to one end of the rope, and fling it over one of the heavy iron bars that criss-cross underneath the pier. Lowering the rock to the ground by slackening the rope, you perform a quick cross-hitch to secure it, and being to climb. A few seconds later, the iron bar - rusted almost right through - breaks, sending you falling back down to the beach, dodging rust and metal shards as you land.
