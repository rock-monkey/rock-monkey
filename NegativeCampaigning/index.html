Negative Campaigning

Negative Campaigning is a shoddy election tactic, the opposite of Positive Campaigning, which involves saying things like "If you vote for us, we will do this, this, and this, and that will be good for you because of this other thing that will happen."
Negative Campaigning, however, involves saying things like "If you vote for them the world will end. And all your children will be Quadruple Flying Bumraped by immigrants who will put you out of work. And the leader of the other party will become PrimeMinister and pimp your mum.
For some reason, Negative Campaigning is becoming ever-more popular in the UK, probably because the Yanks like it too, and it requires far less effor than thinking of ways you could make the voters happy, and the country a better place.
