Yes Minister

A BBC Television Series written by Anthony Jay and Jonathan Lynn.

Details the dealings between the bumbling Jim Hacker (Played by Paul Eddington) and his scheming Permenant Secretary, Sir Humphrey Appleby (played by Nigel Hawthorne) with the Principle Private Secretary, Bernard Woolley (played by Derek Fowlds) caught in the middle.

Bryn Salisbury 's favourite Television show. Considered to be the best textbook on how the British Government actually works.

It's sequel was Yes Prime Minister. Which came about after Sir Humphrey engineers a triumph for Hacker during the "Euro Sausage" debacle.
