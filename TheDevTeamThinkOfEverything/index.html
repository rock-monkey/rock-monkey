The Dev Team Think Of Everything

The Dev Team Think Of Everything, usually abbreviated to TDTTOE, is a phrase used by accomplished NetHack players who are surprised for the millionth time that the development team really do// think of anything. For example, if you eat from a can which turns out to get your hands greasy... but you're currently polymorphed into a creature of a kind that //doesn't have hands, the game (correctly) adapts the sentence to fit (e.g. "Your paws become greasy.", "You now have greasy tentacles."). And if you look into a mirror while hallucinating and polymorphed into a creature with a sight-based petrification ability, you become kind-of entrapped in the gaze of the beast in the mirror, but then it goes away... and... it's just an immensely clever game.
