SettlersOfCatan

SettlersOfCatan is a complex resource-trading/empire-building board game with a modular hex-based board, for 2-4 (2-6 with appropriate expansion packs) players. Expansion packs include:
  • 5/6 Player Expansion
  • CitiesAndKnightsOfCatan
  • 5/6 Player Expansion for CitiesAndKnightsOfCatan
  • SeafarersOfCatan
  • 5/6 Player Expansion for SeafarersOfCatan
And the spin-off games:
  • KidsOfCatan
  • StarfarersOfCatan
It makes a lot of money for MayfairGames.
It is often played at GeekNight.
