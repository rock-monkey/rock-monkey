Settlers Of Catan

Settlers Of Catan is a complex resource-trading/empire-building board game with a modular hex-based board, for 2-4 (2-6 with appropriate expansion packs) players. Expansion packs include:
  • 5/6 Player Expansion
  • Cities And Knights Of Catan
  • 5/6 Player Expansion for Cities And Knights Of Catan
  • Seafarers Of Catan
  • 5/6 Player Expansion for Seafarers Of Catan
And the spin-off games:
  • Kids Of Catan
  • Starfarers Of Catan
It makes a lot of money for Mayfair Games.

It is often played at Geek Night.
