Wales

Part of the UK (despite protestation from some parties) and making up a big bit of it's West coast. Cymru to many of it's inhabitants.

Wales is one of the nicest places on Earth. The people are chilled and friendly, the beer is good and the living is easy. About 20% of the population speak Welsh as a first language, but everybody, almost without exception, speaks English.

We all love Wales and with good reason.

But here at RockMonkey we don't discriminate so we say Nationalism no, nice places yes!
