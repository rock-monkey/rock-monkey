Western Gate

"Well, this is a nice town!" You say breezily, hoping to impress the guard with your easy charm.

The guard turns his head. He is smiling aimlessly. He glances from you to the spike-topped wall, and shakes his head in disgust. Then, abruptly, the inane grin returns. He turns away from you and resumes scratching at the wall with a broken pair of scissors.
