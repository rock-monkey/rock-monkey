Time Porn

Time Porn is a category applied to various TV shows and other media which is enjoyable to watch because it depicts people who have so much more free time than you. So that while you watch you can fantasize about how great it would be to have so little to do.

An example is "Friends", a SitCom in which several friends spend all thier time hanging around in a cafe, lounging in a flat, or otherwise wasting time. Don't these people have jobs???

45 by Bill Drummond is Restless Boy's favourite example of Time Porn - even more satisfying because you know it's non-fiction. The Time Porn equivalent of ReadersWives, except with readers who had really sexy wives. Wives who chopped up artworks and ate a piece of them.
