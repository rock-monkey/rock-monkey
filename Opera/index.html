Opera

Opera is a lovely WebBrowser with some really good skins, a toptastic "personal bar" you can put links to favourite sites on, and a direct link for Google searches in the main toolbar. O, and automatic blocking of those bloody "WARNING! Your Computer Clock May Be Wrong! Click Here For Adware!" pop-ups.
It bills itself as "Simply the best Internet Experience," which is arguably true. It's probably nicer to look at that MozillaFirefox (I think this is subjective - I find Opera too 'busy' to use the interface, and I don't like banner adverts, and I also hate paying for software - JonAtkinson), and it kicks the stuffing out of Internet Explorer ["Simply the Microsoftest Internet Experience"].
