RockMonkey

JoannaNewsom

Deranged gelfling. Plays the harp which makes her fingers bleed. Alternately sounds like a child and an old woman. Sings about mollusc weddings (which are apparently solemn and leaden) and killing her dinner with karate kicks. Released her debut album The Milk-Eyed Mender in 2004. Is absolutely splendid. Much beloved of SianThomas and RestlessBoy.
