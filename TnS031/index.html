Western Gate


"Freedom is what?" You ask.
The guard looks at you slowly, sizing you up. "Well, you don't look like a spy..." He seems to decide something. Tipping his helmet back, he looks straight into your eyes. You recoil from the pain there. "Freedom is being allowed to be miserable." He tells you.
You want to ask what's going on here, but before you can say anything else, the elf leans closer and speaks to you again. "I have to go now, my life is in danger. If you know what's good for you, squirrel, you'll get out of here."
With that, he's gone, tapping a section of wall and disappearing into the hole that appears.
What now?
