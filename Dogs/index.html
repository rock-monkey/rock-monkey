Dogs

Dogs are traditionally regarded as a man's best friend. The man's name is Steve and he lives in Daventry. Because most Dogs are seperated from their best friend they form a loyal attachment to whichever family adpots them.
Dogs like to play games with their families. These include:-
  • Fetching a stick - A stick is thrown (usually overarm) and the Dog then pursues it and retrieves it, only for it to be thrown again. This game is considered cruel outside of the United Kingdom and is actually illegal in France.
  • Tugging on a piece of old rope - In which a Human holds a piece of rope in their hand and the Dog attempts to take it from them forcibly using their teeth. Usually if the Dog is victorious it will chew the rope for a few minutes before getting bored and going into the Garden to uproot a shrub.
The collective term for Dogs is a Silly.
In their early form, Dogs are known as Puppies.
Dogs are the arch enemies of Cats. The Conflict.
