The BigCloud

The BigCloud is a metaphor for all the pages in a wiki which are, by some means or another, linked to one another in the large bulk of the wiki. There are several smaller clouds (usually of only two or three WikiPages each, linked to one another) outlying the BigCloud, and there are a reasonable number of OrphanedPages (WikiPages with no links to them whatsoever) on the outskirts.
The addition of the "Links Here" links on WikiPages and the OrphanedPages/BadPages pages were supposed to alleviate the problem of OrphanedPages, but have met with limited success. There is currently a debate on the OrphanedPagesProblem and how to solve it.
