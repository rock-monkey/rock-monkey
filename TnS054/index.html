Marketplace

You can hear the old elf struggling to get up, so you quickly grab the teapot. Your skin-tight lycra outfit doesn't really leave you with anywhere to hide it, so you put it behind your back.

The elf pops up behind the stall and dusts himself off. Then he looks at you, and there is something piercing about his gaze that makes you think he's not such a fool after all. His eyes flicker to the stall and back to you, but he says nothing about the teapot. "Now then, young squirrel. Can I help you?"
