BadPages

Dan's a big fan of good wiki etiquette, and so these pages are dedicated to helping people better undertand how to make this wiki better for all of us. Here's a list of some of the things that make BadPages bad:
This document is under construction. There's more to come.

1. No Links To Other Pages

Every WikiPage should have at least one, and ideally more, links (or potential links) to related WikiPages. In order to help facilitate this, I've composed a list of pages that violate this principle.
Plenty of cross-linking ensures a vibrant and exciting wiki community, which people can explore for hours without getting stuck 'in a loop' or reaching a DeadEnd.

2. No Content

A WikiPage which is completely blank is even worse than one that has never been defined - it doesn't appear in the WantedPages list. It's surprisingly common to see people deliberately 'blank' pages if they disagree with their content, even though that content can just be 'rolled back' using the 'History' link - far better to provide counter-arguments and update the content you don't appreciate. 3. OrphanedPages

We currently have a problem with orphaned pages (those which are not linked to from anywhere else on the wiki) and subclouds (sections of the wiki not connected to the "BigCloud"). We're debating solutions to the OrphanedPagesProblem.
