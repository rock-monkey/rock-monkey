Lemmings

1. A small furry mammal



Not to be confused with Guinea Pigs, although they are related to hamsters, gerbils and mice. I wonder why we don't keep them as pets?

Don't commit suicide, even though Disney tried to make everyone think they did, although they do tend to fall off cliffs and into lakes due to crowding during mass migration

Don't hibernate, and get eaten by foxes and owls

2. An insanely popular computer game based on the above



Made by DMA Design, who became Rockstar Games and made Grand Theft Auto. Oh well. Came out on the Amiga first.

Quite original because you didn't control the lemmings directly, you could only give orders. Oh, just go and play it...
