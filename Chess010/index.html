You are:
A King!

Sounds pretty cool, huh? Well... not entirely.

If you got to this result because you chose to read a book of some sort then you're someone who is quiet, patient, analytical and willing to perservere to the end. So, quite dull, then.

On the other hand, you could've got here by watching the TV which shows that you're basically lazy, but interested in what's going on around you. You're not entirely bright enough to make connections between what's going on and possible threats to yourself, however. Which is why your Queen is running the country, and everybody's laughing at you behind your back.
Fear not! The best thing about Chess pieces is that their 'personality' differs depending on the person playing with them. Which is why this is a bit of a lame test, really.
