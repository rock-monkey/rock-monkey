Microsoft Access is a database management system produced by Microsoft Corporation and packaged as part of the Microsoft Office suite.
While (occassionally) suitable for small, office applications, it becomes unusable as soon as you try to deploy it in a networked environment, or get it to do anything more simplistic than you could equally achieve with a spreadsheet.
