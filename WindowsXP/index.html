WindowsXP

Remember kids, just say no!

Seriously, it's quite difficult to get a modern PC which doesn't come with it pre-installed. And you payed for that in the price

Why does it always seem to come pre-installed? Well, Microsoft offer a massive discount to manufacturers if all the PCs they sell ship with their Operating System. If even one of them doesn't, Microsoft don't offer the discount. Simple as that. And since most people still want Microsoft Windows (even if it's just for the assurance of something familiar) the retailer doesn't have much of a choice

Will be replaced by Windows Longhorn when they finally get around to releasing it

Quite similar to Windows2000 on the inside, but prettier and with additional features such as a bastardised version of VDMSound

Includes rudimentary DRM and Trusted Computing

No wonder more people are switching to Linux
