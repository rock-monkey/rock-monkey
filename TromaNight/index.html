About TromaNight

TromaNight is a night of the week (usually Saturday) when a group of like-minded friends get together at Dan and Claire's flat to watch films. Sometimes they're AwfulFilms, sometimes they're GoodFilms, and just as often they're MediocreFilms.
The night was named after our love for films by TromaStudios, which are almost all AwfulFilms.
People drink RealAle by default, though some (like JonAtkinson) drink ColdLager. Sometimes people smoke JazzCigarettes.
It's fun.
::Edit - TromaStudios now also seem to be aware of it's existence, which could get interesting....::
