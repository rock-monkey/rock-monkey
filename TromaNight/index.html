About Troma Night

Troma Night is a night of the week (usually Saturday) when a group of like-minded friends get together at Dan and Claire's flat to watch films. Sometimes they're Awful Films, sometimes they're Good Films, and just as often they're Mediocre Films.

The night was named after our love for films by Troma Studios, which are almost all Awful Films.

People drink Real Ale by default, though some (like Jon Atkinson) drink Cold Lager. Sometimes people smoke Jazz Cigarettes.

It's fun.

::Edit - Troma Studios now also seem to be aware of it's existence, which could get interesting....::
