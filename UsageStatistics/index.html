UsageStatistics

RockMonkey now records UsageStatistics, so that the owner can better understand what's going on among the site visitors. Here's some of the more interesting things the logs have revealed:
  • 3rd January 2005: ""BT CentralPlus"" user (using InternetExplorer on Windows XP) searched on AltaVista UK for '""RentBoys""'... and got the second result: RentBoys on RockMonkey. I can't help but feel that this result will have been unsatisfactory, and they were evidently looking for something a little dirtier too, because they then clicked the "Bad Pages" link in the menu, before being unsatisfied there, too, and leaving.
  • 3rd January 2005: ""BT OpenWorld"" user (using InternetExplorer 6 on Windows 98) searched on Google UK for '""jet gladiators games mistress""', using the "pages from the UK" option. This somehow brought them (second result) to our page on Jet. They didn't hang around long after that.
  • 5th January 2005: Mystery user (using InternetExplorer 6 on Windows 98) searched on Yahoo! Search for "what is a tsunami what is a tidalwave". The results in front of them won't have looked that good... except for one about half-way down the results - a page called RockMonkey/Tsunami who's search summary was "Not a TidalWave...". Wow - just what our searcher was looking for; a definition of the difference between the two. They came to our Tsunami page, and sadly discovered that in actual fact, *all* it contained was the text "Not a TidalWave." They clicked the link to TidalWave, hoping that would shed some light on the difference... "Surprisingly a wave caused by tides."
  • 21st January 2005: Thanks to much cross-linking and an unusual word, RockMonkey is now the top web site when searching on Google for Lesbeens.
  • Popular search terms leading people to RockMonkey include: 'thepolice sting', 'hashbrownies', 'mozillafirefox', 'godspeedyoublackemperor', and 'aseriesofunfortunateevents'. Whether these people meant to type WikiWords or just lost the use of thier space bar is not certain.
