RockMonkey

NickDrake


Sadly departed singer-songwriter who went completely unrecognised in his lifetime selling only 5,000 records before overdosing on sleeping tablets in 1976, it is not sure whether his death was suicide or a tragic accident.
Released three albums 'Five Leaves Left', 'Bryter Layter', and 'Pink Moon' and has had unreleased material compiled on 'Time of no reply' and 'Made to love magic'. His subtle guitar playing is amongst the most complicated ever recorded and his voice and lyrics betray youthful melancholy like few before or since.
best album: five leaves left.
