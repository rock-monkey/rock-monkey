Fair Use

Something which the US have a dim view of thanks to the DMCA

Basically, it means you can make a copy for your own use. If you own the CD, you can make a tape of it to put in the car. Or an MP3 to listen to on your iPod

It does NOT mean that you can sell the original. Nice try
