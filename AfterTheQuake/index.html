After The Quake

AfterTheQuake, only people who get sent into the UndergroundBunker will survive.
Note: I made this page using the CategoryPeople page, so anyone who didn't have a page on there at that time wasn't on the list. It's not that I want you to die... well, not necessarily...
Here's how it works:
Everybody gets TWO votes. You can vote for anybody you like from the list below, but remember that only SIX people can be saved. These SIX people will have to repopulate the planet, and survive on what food they can scavenge from the ravaged remains of the planet Earth. If you particularly want to be sent into the bunker and survive the end of the world, you can add a plea to the beginning of your page, in the hope that it will sway people's decision. No negative campaining though, please.
