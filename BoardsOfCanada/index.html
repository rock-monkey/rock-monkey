BoardsOfCanada

Mysterious duo who release dark moody ambient electronica records, packed with subliminal messages and disconcerting effects. Allegedly belong to a small commune/cult called TurquoiseHexagonSun.
Have made two of most stunning and beautiful ambient records ever 'Music has the right to children' and 'Geogaddi'. Both released on WarpRecords.
Unfortunately now best known for "Beware the Friendly Stranger", aka the SaladFingers theme
