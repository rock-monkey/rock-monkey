Boards Of Canada

Mysterious duo who release dark moody ambient electronica records, packed with subliminal messages and disconcerting effects. Allegedly belong to a small commune/cult called Turquoise Hexagon Sun.

Have made two of most stunning and beautiful ambient records ever 'Music has the right to children' and 'Geogaddi'. Both released on Warp Records.

Unfortunately now best known for "Beware the Friendly Stranger", aka the Salad Fingers theme
