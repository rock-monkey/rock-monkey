Collosal Cave

Collosal Cave is a fantasy adventure game of the Interactive Fiction variety that was released in the 1970s and inspired future Interactive Fiction hits such as the Zork series. It was inspited by the "real" Collosal Cave, in Kentucky. It was also known as "Adventure", and became famous for it's illogical puzzles.
