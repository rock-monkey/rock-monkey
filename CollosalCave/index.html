CollosalCave

CollosalCave is a fantasy adventure game of the InteractiveFiction variety that was released in the 1970s and inspired future InteractiveFiction hits such as the Zork series. It was inspited by the "real" CollosalCave, in Kentucky. It was also known as "Adventure", and became famous for it's illogical puzzles.
