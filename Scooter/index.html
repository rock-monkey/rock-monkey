Scooter

Three-piece German Euro Dance group fronted by one H.P. Baxxter

The two backing DJs have changed twice in the fifteen years or so they've been around

H.P. Is insane.

Lyrics consist mostly of references to either themselves, DJs, clubs or TheKLF

Eg:

"Respect to the Man in the Ice-cream van" (Weekend)
"I Want to see your hands up" (Hands up)
"I got some lyrics for you...." (Call Me Manana (live))
"The Painted Cow" (Nessaja)

Recommended listening - Call Me Manana, How Much is the Fish, Ramp (The Logical Song), Weekend and The Night
