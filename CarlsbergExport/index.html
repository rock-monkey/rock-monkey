Carlsberg Export

Carlsberg Export is a crisp, tasty brew, just slightly salty. It's best served very cold, as it loses its flavour at room temperature. Can be bought at Aber Spar for 92p a can. Cheap At Twice The Price.

This is Jon Atkinson's favourite beer. If you want to try some, have a look around The Flat (he usually leaves stray cans around).
