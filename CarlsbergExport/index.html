Carlsberg Export

CarlsbergExport is a crisp, tasty brew, just slightly salty. It's best served very cold, as it loses its flavour at room temperature. Can be bought at AberSpar for 92p a can. CheapAtTwiceThePrice.
This is JonAtkinson's favourite beer. If you want to try some, have a look around TheFlat (he usually leaves stray cans around).
