MicrosoftWorks

MicrosoftWorks is a cut-down version of MicrosoftOffice designed for home and small business usage by MicrosoftCorporation. It consists of a single package which barely does what WordPad can, but claims to be a reasonable replacement to MicrosoftWord, MicrosoftExcel, etc.
Basically, it blows chunks.
