Tax

Tax is a mandatory fee paid to the state by an entity, theoretically in exchange for state services. Taxes in the UnitedKingdom include:
  • IncomeTax
  • CouncilTax (assuming the LiberalDemocrats don't take power)
  • VAT
  • InheritanceTax

In the UK, national taxes are handled by the InlandRevenue.
