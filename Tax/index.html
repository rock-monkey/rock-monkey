Tax

Tax is a mandatory fee paid to the state by an entity, theoretically in exchange for state services. Taxes in the United Kingdom include:
  • Income Tax
  • Council Tax (assuming the Liberal Democrats don't take power)
  • VAT
  • InheritanceTax
In the UK, national taxes are handled by the Inland Revenue.
