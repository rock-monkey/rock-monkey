Autechre

The epitome of cutting edge electronica. Autechre (short for Audio Technology Research and usually pronounced Aw-teck-er) make computer-generated music based on algorythmic patterns. Full of the 'glitchy' sounds which have become the staple of lesser acts they are a difficult listen and their output does tend to blur into one.
Their best album is known variously as 'LP5' 'Autechre' and 'the Black Album' - because it comes in an opaque black plastic jewel case which says 'autechre' on it and nothing else.
Responsibly also for 'Gantz_Graf' a track seemingly consisting of random electronic noise until watched in tandem with its amazing video featuring a shape which moves in response to the music and which reveals the track to be an astoundingly intricate and structured piece.
They were hugely influential on Radiohead's transition into a more electronica-based act.
They are on WarpRecords.
