WatchingPaintDry

WatchingPaintDry was a beautiful ballad about loss and pain, which reached number one for GenericIndieGroup.
Some people condemned it, and the line 'Watching paint dry, and my elbow ran out' sparked rumours that it was written by Igneous, a famous chat-bot, but to the true music conossieur, the sublimeness of the final line transcends all that:
I need to know if you were right, so I'm watching paint dry, to see if it really is more interesting than me.
GenericIndieGroup split up soon after the song was released. Their front-man sobbed openly as he told the press:
"We just knew we'd never write anything so beautiful again."
