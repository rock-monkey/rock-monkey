DMCA

A messy piece of legislature. Europe is quite close to having it's own, but it keeps being overturned
What does it mean for you? Well, basically it means that you're not allowed to circumvent any kind of copy protection on anything you own, even if you want to apply Fair Use laws
This means you can't crack your DigiBox to get free channels. Fair enough
It also means you can't watch your DVDs through anything but an official DVD Player (Official players like Cinemaster, Windvd and Powerdvd pay a license fee). No more Linux player. No more ripping it to your hard drive. That's illegal, dontcha know?
You can't circumvent the protection on Protected Music Cds in order to make an MP3 for your _own use_
Or you'll go to jail. Hooray for the land of the free!
