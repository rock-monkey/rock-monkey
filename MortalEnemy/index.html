Mortal Enemy

I should clarify.

Mortal Enemy doesn't mean you want to 'enemy' someone to death. I mean, that doesn't even work syntatically, so it's not going to work in the real world. What it does mean, is that a Mortal Enemy is someone who keeps you on your toes.

With a Mortal Enemy, someone is there to question everything you do. It's like having a Film Critic on your shoulder. Kind of.
