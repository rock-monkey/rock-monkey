The Method

StephenHawking is the universal constant by which Claire argues that it is possible to determine the difference between a game and a sport: that great mystery. This can be determined, Claire claims, by working out whether or not StephenHawking can participate in it or not.
If he can participate, it is a game; if he cannot, it is a sport. In order to attempt to prove her wrong, RockMonkey hosts two lists:
