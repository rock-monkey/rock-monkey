Stephen Hawking

The Man

Stephen Hawking is one of the world's leading Theoretical Physicists. But you already knew that, because you read about him on Wikipedia.

The Method

Stephen Hawking is the universal constant by which Claire argues that it is possible to determine the difference between a game and a sport: that great mystery. This can be determined, Claire claims, by working out whether or not Stephen Hawking can participate in it or not.

If he can participate, it is a game; if he cannot, it is a sport. In order to attempt to prove her wrong, RockMonkey hosts two lists:
Games Stephen Hawking Cant Play
Sports Stephen Hawking Can Play
In that case why are the OlympicGames so called? (Decides this is final evidence that Claire is wrong.) - Andy Keohane
