By golly we can't be doing without this, now can we? It's integral to Life. It makes almost everything better. When you're sat around not knowing what you need you can bank on the fact that what's missing is a NiceCupOfTeaAndASitDown.
One of the huge factors in this activity is the tea. Please note the abscence of a capital letter -- especially when compared to Coffee -- because it's a laid back drink. You have this to calm the world down. Had AdolfHitler sat down with a good cuppa earlier in his 'career' then who knows what might have gone differently?
Now, as I was saying, tea is essential when enjoying the aforementioned activity and therefore many people seek clarification; what sort of tea? Can we have biscuits? How long should the tea brew? What sort of biscuits can we have? Do we need to use a teapot? We experience drinkers-of-tea and sitters-of-down forgive them their youthful exuberance. Once they've had a NiceCupOfTeaAndASitDown they'll realise the truth, the simple truth: stop fussing, it'll turn out right in the end.
A suggestion on the sitting down side side of things is that you aim more for a slump than a slouch. A good rule of thumb is to imagine that you're visiting older realives; you don't have to be prim and proper but you can't exactly lie down over the sofa. Besides, if you're slouching like that you'll probably spill your tea.
The reason not to spill your tea, aside from the fact that you can't drink what you've spilt down your shirt, is because the tea will be hot. Some people are more familiar with the drink of iced tea <spit>. This drink is an abomination. That's all there is to say about that.
IfYouReadThis you should go and have a NiceCupOfTeaAndASitDown.
