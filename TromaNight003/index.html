Alexandra Terrace

You are on Alexandra Terrace, a long road running East-West alongside the railway station, Somerfield, Wetherspoons, and the Shell filling station. Roads spin off it in many directions. The police station is visible to the Southeast.
What next?
  • Head down the road alongside The Cambrian Hotel, to Cambrian Place
  • Go North along Terrace Road, towards Lloyds TSB
  • Go North into Chalybeate Street, towards The Mecca
  • Walk East towards Holy Trinity Church
  • Go into the Railway Station
  • Go to Somerfield
  • Enter Wetherspoons
  • Go to the Shell filling station
  • Go to the police station
