Aberystwyth Town

Aberystwyth is a small town on the west coast of Wales, at the mouth of the River Ystwyth It may or may not contain Lesbians

Aberystwyth comes from the Welsh for river mouth : "Aber" and twisty turny/ flexible thing : "ystwyth"

Possible translation: "the mouth place of the twisty turny river"
