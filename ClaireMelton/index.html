Claire Melton is a pioneer in the theory that the best way to define the difference between a game and a sport is to determine whether or not Stephen Hawking can participate in it - if he can, it's a game; if he can't, it's a sport. In other to try to prove her wrong, RockMonkey-users are encouraged to contribute to lists of Games Stephen Hawking Cant Play and Sports Stephen Hawking Can Play.
