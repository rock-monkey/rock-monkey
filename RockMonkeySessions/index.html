RockMonkey Sessions

RockMonkey Sessions is a cookie-based technology - provided by the WikiGameToolkit - that allows WikiGame-developers to implement persistance, in the form of "items" (which are carried around with the RockMonkey user) and "tokens" (which are kept invisibley with the the user's session). These can be cleared by the user at any time, and will automatically clear themselves upon logout or after 20 minutes (or thereabouts) of inactivity.
For more information, see WikiGameToolkit.
