MunchkinGame

Munchkin is a custom card game by SteveJacksonGames, somewhat reminiscient of ChezGeek, and played at GeekNight. It is styled on RolePlayingGames, but with strange "munchkinly" rules. In the game, players try to kill monsters and steal their treasure, and to backstab one another in the process. It requires a good deal of co-operation: particularly early in the game (like Hacker, also by SteveJacksonGames), and leads to some hilarious outcomes - "Do not truffle with the humungous fungus!!"
