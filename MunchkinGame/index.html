Munchkin Game

Munchkin is a custom card game by Steve Jackson Games, somewhat reminiscient of Chez Geek, and played at Geek Night. It is styled on Role Playing Games, but with strange "munchkinly" rules. In the game, players try to kill monsters and steal their treasure, and to backstab one another in the process. It requires a good deal of co-operation: particularly early in the game (like Hacker, also by Steve Jackson Games), and leads to some hilarious outcomes - "Do not truffle with the humungous fungus!!"
