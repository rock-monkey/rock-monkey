Scissors, Stone, Paper

You're about to play the Scissors, Stone, Paper WikiGame. Each round, you will opt to throw a "Scissors", "Stone", or "Paper" hand position. The computer will independently do the same.

This WikiGame was made by Dan Huntley, based entirely upon an idea by Matt In The Hat.

Scissors cuts Paper. Paper wraps Stone. Stone blunts Scissors.

Any other result is a tie.
