Winston Churchill

Full title - "(You and Me and) Winston Churchill". A song by Pagan Wanderer Lu.

The kind of record that could ruin your career if you're not careful. Catchy, but releasing it as a single would be a terrible mistake as it doesn't represent his (Pagan Wanderer Lu) typical style of music

Inspired by a bus seen whilst living in Brighton. Not just jolly nonsense, a Google for 'churchill dog' may shed light on the subject matter.
