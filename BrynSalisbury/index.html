Bryn Salisbury is a Computer Science student, back for his final year at the University Of Wales Aberystwyth after a year out at the National Library Of Wales, working, among other things, in Technical Support. He's a regular at Troma Night and Geek Night, and helps run the West Wales Linux User Group, who have a monthly CurryNight.
