Pigs

Highly intelligent pink barnyard-based animals. They are the source of all Bacon in the world, as only they know where it is hidden. Vegetarians consider this exploitation and argue that we should not eat bacon, but should allow Pigs to keep it all for themselves.
The favoured noise of a Pig is 'Oink!'.
A Pig was once narrowly beaten as the Conservative candidate for MP for Daventry, it was felt its position on immigration was not strong enough.
