Western Gate


"I'm on your side. I hate the Queen, and have come to free the elves from her reign of terror!" You tell him. It's a guess, really. You have no idea what's going on.
The elf sneers at you, although his fixed smile remains in place.
"You think you can fool me just by saying that? We'll see who's side you're on!"
He gestures at somebody behind you. You try to turn, but something hits you on the back of the head and you fall unconcious.
