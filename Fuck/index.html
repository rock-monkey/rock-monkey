Fuck

It has been widely-circulated that the swear word Fuck originally came into being as an acronym - "Fornication Under Consent of the King" or "For Unlawful Carnal Knowledge" - as a legal term for talking about pre-marital Sex. This has since been debunked as an UrbanLegend.
While the exact origin of "fuck" is not known, it does come from a Germanic root and cognates can be found in almost all the Germanic languages. Acronymns are pretty much a 20th century invention. The earliest known acronym is ANZAC (Australia-New Zealand Army Corps), dating to the FirstWorldWar. There are some older words where acronymic phrases were created out of existing words, but none that were created from a phrase until the 20th century.
