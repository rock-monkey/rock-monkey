Western Gate


"Ok." You say.
The guard looks furtively about, before coming closer. "Ok," he whispers, "once you've got the salt, look for Topplin' Tom. Tell him that you have the merchandise. He will reply by saying that demand always exceeds supply. You must then say, 'Well, that's life', and he will tell you what to do next. Got that?"
You nod.
He takes something from a pocket and hands it to you. It's a small, brass key. "You will need this to open the boat-house, without it you will never get across the swamp. Good luck!"
He shakes your hand, and then turns back to the wall.
What now?
