RockMonkey

The monkey that rocks. Andy Keohane is such a monkey.
RockMonkey is a derogatory term applied to Geography Students and Earth Science Students, in logic that follows similar reasoning to that of labelling Computer Science Students 'CodeMonkey'. RockMonkeys identify rocks using the highly-advanced Rockipedia.
RockMonkey is also the name of the web site hosted at http://www.rockmonkey.org.uk/ and run by Andy Keohane.
RockMonkey is also the name of a climbing and Outdoor Pursuits group - their web site can be found at http://www.rockmonkey.co.uk/.
