Summary

Reality, as existed in by ComputerScienceStudents (see also LaraCroft) and - alternately - by TheoreticalPhysicists.

But Seriously, Folks...

We were told by the popular media and films like LawnmowerMan that by the year 2000 VirtualReality would be the NextBigThing: whenever we wanted to be entertained we'd just slip on our VR goggles and experience something magical. This has turned out not to be the case, and despite several fields in which VR could be made to be useful, it is not used on account of being 'retro'. It could be said that the VirtualReality revolution came and went in a negative period of time.
