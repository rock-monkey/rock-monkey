But Seriously, Folks...

We were told by the popular media and films like Lawnmower Man that by the year 2000 Virtual Reality would be the Next Big Thing: whenever we wanted to be entertained we'd just slip on our VR goggles and experience something magical. This has turned out not to be the case, and despite several fields in which VR could be made to be useful, it is not used on account of being 'retro'. It could be said that the Virtual Reality revolution came and went in a negative period of time.
