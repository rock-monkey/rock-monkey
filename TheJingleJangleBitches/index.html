The Jingle-Jangle Bitches

Vague idea that Restless Boy has which would require two or three girls with good (but definitely not great), and fairly high pitched voices - and another two or three girls who would be photogenic and could pose with guitars (they could, potentially, be the same girls).

Would be an Imaginary Band (the best kind of of band) with all the music and words written and played by me in the style of late 80s early 90s jangly indiepop but with some really harsh lyrics - no actual songs written yet. I would then post mp3s of their music on the internet and send their recordings to record labels and see what happened.
