The Adventures of Tootie and Sweetie in the Land of the Happy Pixies


A story of love, betrayal and fluffy super-heroes getting horribly mangled.
Tootie and his partner, Sweetie, are ordinary squirrels living in The 6 Acre Wood. At least, that's how it appears by day.
But at night, Tootie and Sweetie are daring crime fighters battling the forces of evil with only their wits and some cool gadgets to help them.

Play as Tootie or Sweetie, and fight to save the Land of the Happy Pixies from its darkest ever threat...
Note: This game uses tokens and items, and therefore you should be logged in before playing
Play as Tootie or Play as Sweetie
