Penglais Hill

You are half-way up Penglais Hill. The entrance to the National Library is on the South side of the road, alongside a short path up to Pantycelyn, a Welsh-speaking hall of residence, and an entrance to Penglais nature reserve is to the North. Looking down into the town, you can just about make out the lights of TheFlat, where TromaNight is to be held. A rusty manhole is set into the pavement.
