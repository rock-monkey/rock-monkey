Microsoft

Microsoft was the name given to a particularly bizzare side-show act which enjoyed vast popularity in Ludlow in the seventeenth century. It consisted of a dwarf with a floppy felt hat sewn to his head; the story went that he'd angered a seamstress by refusing to model for her and she had sewn the hat to him as revenge, finishing with a knot on the inside so the stitches couldn't come unpicked.
In 1974 this was proven to be an elementary fake; the enterprising dwarf had simply built a time machine, travelled to the future, befriended an SF make-up artist, and convinced them to draw on the stitches in a convincing way.
