TheAssembly

Nothing to do with the WelshAssembly
A (failed) plan by Vince Clarke to produce a series of hit singles with a different singer each time
They (Vince and Eric Ratcliffe) produced "Never Ever" with Feargel Sharkey, "One Day" with Paul Quinn and then got bored and formed Yazoo with Alison Moyet instead
