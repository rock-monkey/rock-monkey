Great Darkgate Street

You are on Great Darkgate Street, near Barclays bank. A queue of homeless people is waiting to speak to a white-uniformed UnitedNations officer. Penglais hill looms in the East. From the North, a draught of sea air rolls in, along Terrace Road, before continuing to the South, past Burger King and towards Wetherspoons. Great Darkgate Street heads West, and North Parade extends to the East.
What next?

  • South, down Terrace Road towards Lloyds TSB
  • North, up Terrace Road towards the 24-hour Spar
  • East, along North Parade
  • Talk to the UN Officer
