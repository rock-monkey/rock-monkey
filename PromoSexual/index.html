Promosexuality

The term "PromoSexual", typically used in a derogatory sense, is a play-on-words of the word "HomoSexual". A PromoSexual is one who pretends to be of a different sexuality to that which they are, typically to appear 'cool' or to attract members of thier preferred sex. Stereotypical examples include men pretending to be gay in order to get lots of close female friends, before making thier move, and people in general pretending to be Bi Sexual in order to ride the now-diminishing wave of popular culture that declared it fashionable to "swing both ways".
