MargaretThatcher

In my primary school we used to sing a song about Margaret Thatcher which, in hindsight, made no sense since she was removed from power when I was about 4. Nevertheless this is what we used to sing:
Maggy Thatcher stick her in the bin!
If she gets out then selotape her in
If she gets out then bang her on the head
Then the old bag's dead!
