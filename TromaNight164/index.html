Troma Night Adventure

Roof Of The Students Union Building

You are on the roof of the Students Union building, on Penglais Campus. The view would be great, if it weren't for L'Escala out on the concourse - a large concrete stairwell to nowhere at all. You can clearly see the HughOwen Library and Aberystwyth Arts Centre accross the concourse, as well as some of Penglais Campus's other buildings, such as the Physics Building and Geography Tower.

There's a trapdoor here with a self-locking catch on the top. You could go down through it, but you wouldn't be able to return by that route. And most unexpectedly, there is a manhole here: it has a square cover.
