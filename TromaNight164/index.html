Roof Of The StudentsUnion Building

You are on the roof of the StudentsUnion building, on PenglaisCampus. The view would be great, if it weren't for L'Escala out on the concourse - a large concrete stairwell to nowhere at all. You can clearly see the HughOwen Library and AberystwythArtsCentre accross the concourse, as well as some of PenglaisCampus's other buildings, such as the PhysicsBuilding and GeographyTower.
There's a trapdoor here with a self-locking catch on the top. You could go down through it, but you wouldn't be able to return by that route. And most unexpectedly, there is a manhole here: it has a square cover.
