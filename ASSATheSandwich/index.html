ASSA The Sandwich

Sandwich created by Paul Mann, Andy Keohane, Faye Bromilow, and Bryn Salisbury in MGeesCafe on 30th April 2005.
In the spirit of ASSA the content of the sandwich was decided by each of the four participants contributing one of the fillings without knowledge of what the others had picked.
Hence the sandwich contained Salmon, Sweetcorn, Lettuce and Cranberry.
