SpiritedAway

An AnimeFilm about a girl who's parents turn into pigs. Probably the best moment? When the Little Girl is flying on the back of the Dragon, and tells it its name. At which point, the Dragon turns back into a Human Male Thing, and loses its ability to fly. So the Little Girl and the former Dragon plummet. Genius on the part of the Little Girl.
