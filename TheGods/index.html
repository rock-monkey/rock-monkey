The Gods

Omnipresent beings in stories such as Discworld who stay largely out of the picture, except to intervene in exceptional circumstances. This is in sharp constract to most human Gods, who are supposedly frequently called upon and involved in everyday life.
Also, the name of the third (or sometimes fourth) gallery up in big theatres. The Gods is a long way away from the action, and because their basically level with the ceiling the people sitting there can't really tell what's happening on stage. Why it's called The Gods, I wouldn't deign to guess.
