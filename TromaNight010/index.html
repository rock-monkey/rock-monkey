Troma Night Adventure

Shell Filling Station

You find yourself in the forecourt of the Shell filling station. People are filling up their cars with fuel. On the other side of a glass hatch, an attendant in his early twenties is becoming more and more frustrated with having to push stupidly-shaped items through the slot that seperates him from his customers.

There's a manhole-style hatch near the fire extinguishers.
