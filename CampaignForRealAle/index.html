Campaign For Real Ale

The Campaign For Real Ale, or CAMRA, has a website at http://www.camra.org.uk and is dedicated to the preservation of honest Real Ale, as opposed to Lager, the watery overchilled pap - manufactured cheaply and padded-out with rice alcohol - drunk by a Lager Boy.

The Campaign For Real Ale has more than 50,000 members, including Mister J T A and Ruth Varley who signed up because they like Real Ale, they were bored, and because Robin Varley was around, and would have objected to their having Noisy Nookie on the sofa.
