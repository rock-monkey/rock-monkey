Berk

Berk is a SwearWord which is most certainly British. Based on Cockney RhymingSlang this insult is derived, somewhat dubiously from the term "Berkshire Hunt," which rhymes with the universal SwearWord cunt.
Often used where a "cunt!" is not appropriate, as in "Sorry I'm late, your majesty, that Berk Earl Spencer boxed my car in."
