Western Gate

You are at one of the town gates. To the East is the marketplace where you first arrived, to the west is a dirt track leading into the swamp. To north and south the town wall stretches on; although it is 15 feet high and topped with spikes and shards of broken glass, somebody has painted it sunshine yellow with stencils of little pink pigs.
