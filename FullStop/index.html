The National Full Stop Shortage

There is a Nationwide shortage of FullStops, as detailed on Dan's weblog Here at RockMonkey we are deeply affected by this tragic loss, and - while we have plenty of FullStops ourselves, we will nonetheless ration our Full Stop usage in sympathy of those less fortunate than ourselves who do not even have enough full stops to write a novella

Dan Huntley is encouraging all RockMonkey members to join his Full Stop Rationing campaign in order to conserve our planet's depleting supply of FullStops - please read what you can do to help
