There is a Nationwide shortage of FullStops, as detailed on Dan's weblog Here at RockMonkey we are deeply affected by this tragic loss, and - while we have plenty of FullStops ourselves, we will nonetheless ration our FullStop usage in sympathy of those less fortunate than ourselves who do not even have enough full stops to write a novella
