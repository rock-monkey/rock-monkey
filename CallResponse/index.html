Call Response

A fun kind of music, where one singer say something, and the other sings something which relates to the first singer. This can be spoken, or the second singer can even be replaced by a riff. A variation is "Question & Answer", where the two lines take on special meaning

Example includes:

David Bowie - The Laughing Gnome

"Haven't you got an 'ome to go to?"
'No, we're gnomads'
"Didn't they teach you to get your hair cut at school? You look like a rolling gnome."
'No, not at the London School of Ecognomics'
(This isn't strictly Call and Response but we'll let it go...)

TheBeatles were quite fond of this. As are ASSA.
