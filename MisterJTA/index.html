Mister J T A

Mister J T A goes out with Ruth Varley. Sometimes she scares him. More often than that, though, she'll hit him, because of something SomeOne has said. Or something Dan Huntley has pretended he said, even when he didn't.

Mister J T A is an English Student or Word Monkey at the University Of Wales Aberystwyth and likes wearing waistcoats. He is a member of CAMRA, the Campaign For Real Ale, having been weaned off cider by himself, and off Guinness by Ruth Varley.

Mister J T A was once the victim of an Assassination Attempt, but managed to survive, after a bottle of ale effectively took the bullet for him.

He is only very slightly scared of his girlfriend, most of the time.

He plays a significant amount of NetHack, much to Ruth's displeasure, and forms one half of TheStattoJTAPublishingCorporation.
Wise Words From Mister J T A's Little Red Book:

  • "I'm so off-the-wall I'm on-the-ceiling."
  • "Most of my relationships are just ships that sink in the night..."
