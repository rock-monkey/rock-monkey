You're on upper Great Darkgate Street, outside The Acadamy and near to the market hall. Down a slight gradient, to the East, lies the rest of Great Darkgate Steet and central Aberystwyth. To the West, you can clearly make out the remaining freestanding tower of Aberystwyth Castle. Pier Street leads Northwest, towards the pier and the sea (unlike Morcambe, Aberystwyth's pier spends most of it's time actually over water, albeit not over much water on account of it's diminuitive size). Bridge Street heads South, towards the marina.
