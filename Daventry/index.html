Daventry

Daventry is a fictional town inhabited by Strokey Adam, StrokeyEmma and lots of Guinea Pigs.
It is home to the largest PaperClip factory in the WesternHemisphere, where the paperclips are bent into shape by Guinea Pigs. One naughty GuineaPig called Jim, went through a phase of making swearwords out of the paperclip wire. He was not dismissed but rather became the guru of those Chinese Guys In The Street Making Your Name Out Of Wire, who travel from the FarEast to study under him before going out into the world.
Swear Words and PaperClips are Daventry's two primary exports. The word Quim Ninja was the most recent swearword to be created in Daventry, it was not as succesful as previous efforts such as KnobJockey, TwatGoblin and FuckBadger.
It is also home to the famous Daventry Moo Cow Orchestra.
It was the fourth in a series of 26 planned towns built by the late Dave Entry. The first two Aventry and Baventry, blew away in a high wind one spring morning. The third, Coventry is still standing and was awarded non-fictitious status by Sir Margaret Thatcher in 1985. The series only got as far as Eventry, but no one has ever been there as Dave won't tell anyone where it is.
It is twinned with the French town of Deriere.
Famous people born in Daventry include MartinBashir and Kermit The Frog.
Also the name of a fictional SexualPosition.
