Bi Sexual

Sexuality waning in popularity as the fad comes to an end (see PromoSexual).
A person who is bisexual is sexually attracted to members of either sex. This can lead to a great deal of confusion, particularly by people who decide that this means that they "can't make up thier minds" or assume that they are therefore actually gay or straight.
WoodyAllen said, "Bisexuality immediately doubles your chances for a date on Saturday night." This statement fails to take in to account...
  • Basic mathematics: it would be true only if everybody you knew was a viable date - however, statistically, most people are Hetero Sexual, which dramatically reduces the number of people of your own gender who might want to go out with you anyway.
  • Human nature: the things a Bi Sexual person finds attractive in one gender may be entirely different to those they find attractive in another. This may reduce the potential 'pool' of candidate dates from either or both genders.
  • Troma Night: as everybody knows, you shouldn't be looking for a date on Saturday nights, because you'll end up bringing them along to Troma Night, and as Liz will testify: this is a quick way to scare a date off.
  • And you can't double zero :-)
