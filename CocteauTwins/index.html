Cocteau Twins


Blissed out ambient rock-trio fronted by Liz Fraser who's now best known for singing on the Lord Of The Rings soundtrack. Truly strange and wonderful band who split in 1996.

best albums - 'Treasure' and 'Heaven or Las Vegas'.
