MiltonSirotta

MiltonSirotta is a type of English cheese made in Daventry from GuineaPig milk. It is much prized by connoisseurs for it's light creamy texture and trademark flecks of sawdust.
Hard to get hold of at the moment due to the GuineaPigStrike, and even quite low-grade specimens (cut with rat milk) reach quite high prices on the black market.
It is best eaten with radish fritatters or as a light sauce to go with Pizza. The best wine to drink with it is probably a nice vintage Chateau de Cochon Guinea.
