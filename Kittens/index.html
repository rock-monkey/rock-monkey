Kittens

(sometimes spelled as Kittins)

Younger version of Cats. Kittens elicit high-pitched squealing and irrational behaviour from otherwise sensible Humans. Especially if given balls of Wool.

Kittens get on just fine with Puppies despite the usual rivalry between their two species.
