David Lynch

Arty yet popular film director. Responsible for Twin Peaks, Eraserhead, Dune, The Elephant Man, Lost Highway, Mullholland Drive, Wild at Heart and Blue Velvet.
Known for confusing off-beat storylines and sinister characters, most of his films are actually perfectly comprehensible but wonderfully bizarre. If you dont want your head to hurt then avoid Eraserhead, Lost Highway and Mullholland Drive. But you should want your head to hurt.
