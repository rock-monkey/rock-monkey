Stella Artois

StellaArtois is a Belgian beer, usually around 6.5%. It has a very strong flavour, and it's quite fizzy. It also takes quite a while to settle. It is often responsible for the worst type of hangover, the "stangover".
Known in many northern towns as "wife-beater". It's not uncommon to hear people at the bar make requests such as "a pint of wife-beater, mate". This is presumably because the highly alcoholic nature of Stella, which makes people violent towards their spouses. I can't confirm this. Honest.
