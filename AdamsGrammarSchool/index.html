Adams Grammar School

Adams Grammar School, or AGS, as it is also known, is the place Mister J T A was taught stuff from 1996-2003. Mostly he learned things like "maroon blazers don't do you any favours of buses full of people who hate you for the uniform," giving him a paranoid streak that's still not faded.
The Big School building was constructed in 1656, and is listed as being Grade II. Mister J T A did not know this when he carved his initials into the stonework, and has had to promise the bursar he'll give money to the appeal fund when he is Rich And Famous.
Mister J T A believes that, as schools go, it's a pretty sodding good one, to judge by what other people say of their school lives.
