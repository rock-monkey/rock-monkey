VarsityChristmasCrackerPullingCompetition

Competition held in 1599 between FenlandPolytechnic and the UniversityofOxford during which teams from both institutions pulled Christmas crackers in a bid to find out who was best at it. It was held at FenlandPolytechnic.
The competition, originally intended to be an annual affair, was pulled (if you'll pardon the pun) in the year of its inception because creating the crackers and associated gifts for the match bankrupted five OxbridgeColleges.
Notable prizes include:
  • The town now attached to FenlandPolytechnic, known as Cambridge.
  • OliverCromwell, who was to become LordProtector of England in the farce that was the EnglishCivilWar.
