Vegetarian

The name for a person who choses to abstain from eating meat. Vegetarians include:
Ruth Varley
Kt Errington
Sian Thomas
Sundeep B
In addition to this it is also possible to be a PacifistVegetarian, apparently.
'Vegetarian' is also a term used to describe the type of food (e.g pizza) which is suitable for consumption by the above individuals. Vegetarian Pizzas are ordered at Troma Night, but those who order them open themselves up for mockery by carnivores who are intolerant of different lifestyle choices.
