Chalybeate Street

You are on Chalybeate Street, between The Mecca coffee house and the hardware store. To the North you can see Great Darkgate Street and NatWest bank. To the South is Alexandra Terrace - you can see the Shell filling station and the roundabout. To the East is CambrianPlace and, of course, TheFlat.
