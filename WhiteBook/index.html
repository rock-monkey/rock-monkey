White Book

One of the Rainbow Books
This one describes how to make a VideoCd.
Mode 2/XA MPEG-1 encoded video at 352x288/25fps or 352x240/29.97fps at 1.13Mbit/s, with 224kbit 44.1khz/16-bit/2 channel MPEG-1 layer 2 audio to be precise
Includes support for additional language tracks, but no-one ever uses them. Except for a couple of karaoke CDs maybe
Can also include a primitive one-level menu system
