Soft Synth

What are the two biggest problems with synthesizers?
They're big. You only have so much space
They're expensive. You can only afford a couple, typically
A Soft Synth is a PC emulation of a real synthesizer. Propellorhead Reason is the best known example of one. Features a dozen or so real-world synthesizers. Click the buttons, drag the knobs, connect cables as if it were real. Scary stuff...
No, there is no such thing as an analogue Soft Synth
