Black Smoke

Smoke of a darkish hue.
The Yirrik, a race of half-dwarf half-rat creatures who live under Penbryn and feed on Branwen's leftovers, believe that Black Smoke has many fantastic and mystical powers, including the ability to allieviate boredom and induce a condition not unlike coughing.
But then, they aren't real, so they'd probably believe anything.
Also a spin-off from TheKLF involving Jimmy Cauty. Seem to release MP3s from their website, then pull them between 24-hours and a week later. At the moment, they've pulled the
entire website
