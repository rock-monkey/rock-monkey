ChanServ

ChanServ is a bot on irc.freenode.net (the server where the RockMonkey Chat Room is currently hosted). It's purpose is to keep a list of active channels and keep them alive for their registered owners.

It's also there to give OperatorStatus to the channel admins and enforce any channel bans.
