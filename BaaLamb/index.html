Baa Lamb

Baa-Lambs

Smaller more cute version of Sheep. They frolic and gambol through the grass in springtime and distress hillwalkers by being hit by cars then dragging themselves off to die in a sad, bloody heap in the middle of a field.

Restless Boy once wept at the sight of a dead Baa Lamb.
