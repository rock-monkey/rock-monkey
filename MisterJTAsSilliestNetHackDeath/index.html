MisterJTAsSilliestNetHackDeath

MisterJTA was playing NetHack as JTA CLVIII, a lawful female dwarven valkyrie having died many times before. Wielding the Blessed Rustproof +2 Mjollnir he ventured down to Level 17 of the Dungeons of Doom, but could not complete the GnomishMines.
Finding a Scroll of Genocide, he chose to wipe out all Dwarves.
He died, and his tombstone, never discovered, probably reads "Genocided by a Berk".
