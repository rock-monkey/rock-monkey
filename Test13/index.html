RockMonkey Personality Test: Question 5

The world has just ended, and you are standing before Anubis, god of the dead (yeah, turns out the Egyptians were right - who'd have thought it?). He's just condemned you to spend eternity in the fiery pit. Why?

a) Because you lived a thoroughly disreputable life. Oh well.

b) He hasn't got a reason. Everybody picks on you!

c) You called him a 'dog-faced twat'. You didn't know he was a god!

d) You couldn't afford the bribe.

e) You asked him to. All your friends were going there.
