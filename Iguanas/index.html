Iguanas


_so_ cute!
Don't survive well in the Welsh climate though, otherwise PaulMann would have one as a pet. Latin name for the green iguana is "iguana iguana"
Around 2 metres in length, weigh up to 5kg. They do _not_ eat insects, as is popularly believed. Can fall from great heights, and can fun faster than you'd think. Considered "threatened" but not "endangered"
