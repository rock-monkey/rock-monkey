Troma Night Adventure

Railway Station



As you push on, you catch a glimpse of Mister J T A. He's fighting the crowd, too: trying to get to the other exit. You shout out to him, but he doesn't hear you...

Suddenly, you're pushed over, and you feel the feet of the crowd pounding around you. You roll aside and crawl on, dodging legs as you head towards the last position you saw JTA. There's a break in the crowd, and you take your opportunity, leaping up and over a group of schoolchildren and landing hard on the concrete on the other side.

You stand up and dust yourself off. Mister J T A is stood in front of you - you successfully escaped the crowd and found him. As the crowd disperses in it's many directions, you realise that you've just managed to find one of the Troma Night crew.

What next?
